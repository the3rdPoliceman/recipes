Prawn & Basil Pasta
Ingredients
Olive oil
Prawns
1/2 clove Garlic
Basil
Pine Nuts
Method
Make a loose pesto with the Basil, Oil and some Salt & Pepper
Toast Pine Nuts
Fry the Prawns and Garlic together, until the Prawns are cooked
Mix together Prawns, Pesto, most of the Pine Nuts and Pasta.
Chuck some Pine Nuts on top with a bit of Basil when serving
