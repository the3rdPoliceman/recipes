Agnolotti
Ingredients
225g Flour
1 Egg
6 Egg Yolks
5g olive oil
15g milk
Filling
Directions
NOTE: try with more whole eggs instead of all yolks
Make a well from the flour and put every thing in the middle
Whisk it around with your fingers gradually incorporating everything
Knead, by pushing it around a lot, rather than doubling over like with bread dough
When rolling out, ideally go a bit past the second last width setting on the pasta machine. The thinnest is too delicate, the second last is too thick
