Garlic and Olive Oil Spaghetti
Ingredients
450 g Spaghetti
90ml extra virgin olive oil
3 Cloves Garlic, very finely chopped
1-2 Red Chillis (depends how hot they are)
A bunch of parsley
Directions
Add Oil, Garlic and Chilli to a saucepan, and heat gently until garlic has just a hint of color
Cook the spaghetti, mix in the oil, and parsley
