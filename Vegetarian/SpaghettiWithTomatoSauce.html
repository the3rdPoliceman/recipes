Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce
Ingredients
2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
8 plum tomatoes1 tin whole tomatoes
Pinch of red chili flakes
60ml extra virgin olive oil
6 cloves of garlic, whole
2 sprigs of basil, leaves on
Pinch of red chili flakes
8 basil leaves
25g butter, unsalted
4 tablespoons grated Parmesan
Directions
Skin the tomatoes, separate the flesh, and reserve the seeds. Heat 2 tablespoons of Oil in a saucepan and add the Tomatoes along with the juice from the Seeds, a good bit of Salt and a pinch of Chilli Flakes. Mash them a bit, and leave to bubble for 30 minutes or so until they get to a sauce consistency
Next, make the basil and garlic oil. Add the Oil, Garlic, Basil, and Chilli Flakes to a pan and heat very slowly until the Garlic is very slightly browned (around 20 minutes)
Strain the Oil and stir into the Tomato Sauce. Season with Salt
To Complete
Cook Spaghetti until almost done. Reserve some of the pasta water and drain. Add enough Tomato Sauce to a pan, add in the Spaghetti and some of the reserved pasta water. Cook until the sauce has a good consistency, then add the chopped basil, Parmesan, and Butter. Mix well and serve
