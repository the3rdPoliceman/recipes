Directions
Make a 3:1 dressing with the Lemon and Oil
Add Chickpeas, Onion, Chili and Tomatoes in a bowl, and add enough dressing to make it a bit sloppy at the bottom
Before serving, check seasoning for Pepper, add the chopped herbs, and crumble in the Feta
