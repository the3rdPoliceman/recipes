Saag Paneer
Ingredients
vegetable oil
300g Paneer
400g spinach
1 medium white onion, sliced
1 thumb ginger, grated
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp turmeric
1 tbsp cumin seed
1 tbsp mustard seeds
1/2 tsp cayenne
1/2 tsp garam masala
2 teaspoons ground coriander
150ml full cream
Directions
Dice the paneer and simmer in salted water to soften
Add the onions, garlic, ginger and fry with oil, until golden
add the spices and toast withh a bit more oil until aromatic
Add the spinach and warm, adding enough cream to make it currylike
Add the drained paneer
