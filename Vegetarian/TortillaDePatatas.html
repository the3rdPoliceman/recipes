Tortilla de Patatas
Gypsy Chef
Ingredients
Potatoes
1 medium onion
10 eggs
Olive Oil
Directions
Peel the potatoes and use a knife to lever off chunks of the potato rather than chop them
Chop the onion into 1cm dice and put in a bowl with the potatoes and all the salt you're going to use for the recipe.
Set aside for 20 minutes to let the water out of the potatoes. Get rid of the water (maybe dry the potatoes and onion also?)
Heat enough olive oil to fry the potatoes. It should be hot enough that th oil bubbles when a piece of potato is put in, but does not go crazy
Add the potatoes and onion and cook until potatoes are tender and onion soft
Turn up the heat a bit at the end to give th potatoes a slight golden edge (15-20 mins total)
Crack three or four eggs into a bowl, mix the drained potatoes into it (a bit of oil does no harm), and stir well until it goes creamy as the potatoes cook the eggs
Put in the rest fo the eggs and stir some more
To cook the final tortilla, get a pan very hot and add a few tablespoons of the cooking oil from before
Add the mix, and flip around like an omlette, until it's gets close to cooked enough. The let settle in the pan so the bottom cooks solid
Drip some oil over the top, flip and cook the other side, then serve
