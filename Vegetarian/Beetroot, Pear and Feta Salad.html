Beetroot, Pear and Feta Salad
Ingredients
1 Small Uncooked Beetroot
1 Pear
50g Feta
Mint
1 Lemon
Olive Oil
Directions
Make a 3:1 Dressing with the Oil and Lemon, adding some Pepper
Very thinly slice the Pear and Beetroot (Veggie Peeler works well). Mix it with a handful of chopped Mint, and some dressing
Add the Feta on top when serving
