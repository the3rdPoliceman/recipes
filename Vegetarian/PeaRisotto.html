Pea Risotto
Ingredients
200g peas
Prosciutto
Mint Oil
Directions
Cook the risotto as normal
While the risotto is cooking, cook the peas in salted water and drain
Blitz half the peas along with 50g butter to a puree
When the risotto is done, beat in the parmesan, pea puree and whole peas
As a garnish, add some finely sliced ham, or mint oil
