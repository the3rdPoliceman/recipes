Celeriac Puree
Ingredients
1 medium Celeriac
Â½ Medium Onion
50 ml Cream
Butter
Chicken Stock
Method
Fry the Onion until soft but not coloured.  Add the Celeriac and fry for one minute, then add the Stock. 
Bring to the boil, simmer until the Celeriac is tender, and drain (reserving the Stock).
Place the Celeriac and Onion mixture into a food processor with a few tablespoons of the reserved Stock and 25-50g Butter and blend until smooth. 
Add the Cream and season.
