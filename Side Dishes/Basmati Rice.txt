Basmati Rice
Ingredients
Basmati Rice
Water
Method
Keys: 4:3 water to rice ratio by volume, or 7:4 by weight  
Rinse, Soak, Oven.
Rinse the rice in a sieve and soak it in a bowl of cold water for half an hour. 
Drain and add to a pan along with 1.33X ratio of water to rice (dry rice volume).
Season and bring the to the boil, then put a tight fitting lid on.
Put the covered pan in the oven set to 180C for 20 minutes
