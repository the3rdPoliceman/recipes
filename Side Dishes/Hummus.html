Hummus
Ingredients
1 can chickpeas
½ teaspoon baking soda
60ml lemon juice
1 clove garlic
½ teaspoon salt
120g tahini
2 tablespoons ice water (may need a bit more)
½ teaspoon ground cumin
1 tablespoon olive oil
Directions
Rinse the chickpeas, then put in a saucepan with the baking soda and enough water to cover the chickpeas by 5cm
Boil them for 20 minutes
Meanwhile, combine the lemon juice, garlic and salt in a blender until the garlic is very finely chopped, then let the mixture rest so the garlic flavor can mellow - at least 10 minutes
Add the tahini and blend until thick and creamy
While running the food processor, drizzle in the ice water and blend until very smooth
Once the chickpeas are done, drain and put back in the pan with cold water. Give them a rub to release the chickpea skins, and scoop the skins out of the water (No need to get them all). Drain them again
Add the chickpeas, cumin and olive and blend again until done
Add more salt and lemon if needed
