Green Bean and Potato Curry
Ingredients
oil
1/2 tsp mustard seeds
1/4 tsp onion powder
3 cloves garlic sliced
500g green beans, chopped to 3cm
4 small potatoes in small cubes
1/2 tsp ground turmeric
1/4 tsp cayenne powder
2.5 tsp ground coriander , see notes
1/2 tsp ground cumin
salt
fresh lemon juice
fresh coriander
Method
Heat the cooking oil, then add the mustard seeds, onion powder, and garlic and fry for a few seconds. 
Add the beans, potatoes, turmeric, chili powder and salt. Fry for a few minutes. 
Add half a cup of water, cover the pan, and cook on medium-low heat until the potatoes and green beans are cooked and tender. Stir occasionally, and add more water if needed a little at a time.
Once the green beans and potatoes are cooked, add the ground cumin and ground coriander.
Mix well and cook uncovered on medium heat for an additional 2-3 minutes until any liquid is gone. 
Add salt to taste and some lemon juice and fresh coriander.
