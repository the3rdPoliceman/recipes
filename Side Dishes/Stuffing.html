Stuffing
Ingredients
40g butter
100g shallots or onion
2 celery stalks, finely chopped
turkey liver, finely chopped
500g pork sausagemeat
grated zest of 2 lemons
100g fresh breadcrumbs
75g almonds, finely chopped
Bunch of chopped fresh parsley
1/2 tsp dried thyme
salt and pepper
Directions
Fry onion and shallots in butter
Allow to cool, then mix everything together.
Cook.
