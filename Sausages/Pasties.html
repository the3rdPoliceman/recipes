Pasties
Ingredients
200g cooked sausage meat
150g mashed potatos
1 small onion
1 pinch of nutmeg
1 pinch of Mace
1 pinch of allspice
1 pinch of corriander
1 pinch of ground ginger
1 pinch of ground cloves
Lots of Pepper
self raising flour
soda water
Directions
Combine everything up to the pepper, form patties, dust with flour and freeze
Make batter with self raising flour and soda water
Deep Fry
