Lemon & Thyme Sausages
Ingredients
2.75Kg Pork Voressen  (guess it was 20% Fat)
300g Pork Backfat
22ml Black Pepper
40g Salt
250ml White Wine
Diced Peel from 6 small preserved lemons
30ml Thyme leaves
Method
Standard sausage method
Minced everything through large die, might be better to use medium die for at least some of the mix.
