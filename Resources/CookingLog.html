Crepes
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V_okk5pOLp4
Ingredients:
20g Butter
50g Flour
1 egg
pinch salt
5g sugar
some milk

Method:
Melt butter in the pan
Put everything else, but only a bit of the milk in a bowl and mix until smooth
Add the rest of the milk, enough to make it thin
Dump the melted butter in to the batter
Mix again, then cook your crepes
When cooking a crepe, dump the batter in one side of the pan and let it flow to the other, rather than dumping in the middle

Result:
Good. Tried with thick chocolate spread - about 1 part cream, 2 parts chocolate melted and stirred together

08/09/2020
Beetroot Cured Salmon
300g salmon
1/2 small beetroot
Zest of 1 orange
Zest of 1 lemon
Large bunch of dill
32g kosher salt
30g sugar

Method:
Mix everything bar the dill and coat the salmon with the mixture.
Refrigerate for 24 hours, covered in plastic and weighted down with something.
Rinse the cure away, and let sit another half day in the fridge to allow salt to redistribute a bit.
Cover with finely chopped dill and serve.

Result:
Too salty, and not sure the beetroot brings much except color.
Should try again reducing either the salt or the cure time. (serious eats cure is 40g salt 25g sugar 900g salmon for 2 days)
White pepper is also a common addition. Coriander may also be good
