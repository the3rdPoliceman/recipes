Tiramisu
My Recipe
Trying my version of La Cucina Italia's tiramisu, with less egg yolk and sugar. Used 100ml coffee per 100g biscuits as it was hard to make them soak less.

3 egg whites
2 egg yolks
55g Sugar
250g Mascarpone
1 tbsp Gran Marnier
100g Savoiardi Biscuits
100ml strong coffee
Unsweetened cocoa powder

Result:
Sugar was better. Could go up to 60g
Less egg yolk seemed good - maybe now there's less sugar it would be worth trying again with the extra yolk
100ml coffee to 100g biscuits was too much. Coffee probably not strong enough
Could have few more biscuits too. Probably 150g biscuits to 100ml coffee would be good

08/09/2020
Beetroot Cured Salmon
300g salmon
1/2 small beetroot
Zest of 1 orange
Zest of 1 lemon
Large bunch of dill
32g kosher salt
30g sugar

Method:
Mix everything bar the dill and coat the salmon with the mixture.
Refrigerate for 24 hours, covered in plastic and weighted down with something.
Rinse the cure away, and let sit another half day in the fridge to allow salt to redistribute a bit.
Cover with finely chopped dill and serve.

Result:
Too salty, and not sure the beetroot brings much except color.
Should try again reducing either the salt or the cure time. (serious eats cure is 40g salt 25g sugar 900g salmon for 2 days)
White pepper is also a common addition. Coriander may also be good
