Beetroot Cured Salmon
300g salmon
1/2 small beetroot
Zest of 1 orange
Zest of 1 lemon
Large bunch of dill
32g kosher salt
30g sugar
Method:
Mix everything bar the dill and coat the salmon with the mixture.
Refrigerate for 24 hours, covered in plastic and weighted down with something.
Rinse the cure away, and let sit another half day in the fridge to allow salt to redistribute a bit.
Cover with finely chopped dill and serve.

Result:
Too salty, and not sure the beetroow brings much except color. Should try again reducing either the salt or the cure time.
