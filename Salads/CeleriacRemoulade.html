Celeriac Remoulade
Ingredients
1 Large Celeriac
120ml Mayo
120ml Creme Fraiche
2Tbsps Cornichons
2Tbsps Capers
2tsps Dijon
1tbsp Cider Vinegar
3tbsps in total of tarragon chervil parsley and chives
Lemon
Directions
Mix everything together except the Celeriac and Lemon and season. Put in fridge for 1hr-3days, the flavors will improve
Chop the Celeriac into matchsticks or grate finely, add enough dressing to coat and refrigerate for 2 to 8 hours
To serve, add more Dressing, Seasoning and Lemon as you like
