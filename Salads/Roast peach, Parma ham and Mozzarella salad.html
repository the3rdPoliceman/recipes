Roast peach, Parma ham & Mozzarella salad
From Jamie Oliver
Ingredients
6 peaches
extra virgin olive oil
2 X 125 g balls of buffalo mozzarella
12 slices of Parma ham
1 lemon
sea salt
freshly ground black pepper
100 g rocket
a few sprigs of fresh mint , leaves picked
Directions
Roast peaches for 20 minutes with a little oil. Make vinaigrette with lemon and oil, add to rocket, and chuck everything else on top
