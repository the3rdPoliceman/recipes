Zuni Chicken Legs
Chicken Legs
Thyme
Salt
  1. Poke a finger between the skin and meat each leg and put a sprig of thyme in there
  2. Salt the chicken legs using 6g salt for 4 legs
  3. Refrigerate for 2 days
  4. Grill with lid on highest heat
