Baba Ganoush
1kg aubergines
50g tahini
80ml extra-virgin olive oil
1 small bunch of parsley
30g yogurt
1-2 cloves garlic, crushed
25-40ml lemon juice
salt
pinch of cumin
pinch of cayenne
Try smoked salt
Try some yoghurt mixed in (seems to be quite common in traditional recipes). Up to 100g
Gets better after a day or 2
  1. Burn the aubergine in the oven or grill until they're very soft inside and charred outside
  2. Scrape the flesh out and remove excess water. This can be done by leaving in a sieve for half an hour or gently spinning in a salad spinner
  3. Mix in the other tahini, oil, joghurt and parsley
  4. Then add salt, garlic and lemon to taste
  5. Drizzle with olive oil when serving
