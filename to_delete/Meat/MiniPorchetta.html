Porchetta
1kg Schweinehals
1.5tsp Salt
1 tablespoon capers, rinsed and dried
zest of 1 lemon
3 cloves of garlic, roughly chopped
12 sage leaves, chopped
2 sprigs of rosemary, chopped
2 tsp fennel seeds, crushed
1.5 tsp black pepper
5 slices fatty bacon
Try with fennel flowers(fiori di Finocchio) instead of seeds. Suggested by good sources
  1. Keep the pork in one piece, but cut into and follow the fat seams to create a lot of surfaces for seasoning
  2. Salt all the surfaces
  3. Mix together all the other ingredients except the bacon, and put them in the cavities you just cut
  4. Reshape the pork, arrange the bacon on top, and tie it with butchers string
  5. Wrap it u and leave for 2 days in the fridge
  6. Cook at around 180C until it gets near 85C (2-2.5hrs)
