Momofuku Octo Vinaigrette
Ingredients
2 tbsp garlic
2 tbsp ginger
1 chilli
60ml rice wine vinegar
60ml light soy sauce
2 tbsp neutral oil (sunflower/rapeseed etc)
1/4 tsp sesame oil
1 tbsp sugar
black pepper
Directions
Chop the garlic, ginger and chilli into ~2mm cubes
Mix everything together. Taste and add a bit more sugar if it needs it
