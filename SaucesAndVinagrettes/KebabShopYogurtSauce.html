Kebab Shop Yogurt Sauce
Ingredients
50g mayonnaise
120g greek yogurt
12g sugar
30g white vinegar
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 bunch of chopped parsley
1 tsp ground black pepper
salt
Directions
original recipe called for 2tsp black pepper, so try one first then add more as necessary
not tested this recipe
Whisk everything except the salt together, the season with the salt to taste
