Amaretto & Walnut Cake
Video from River Cafe
Ingredients
Cake
300g softened butter
300g fine sugar
4 large eggs
2.5 vanilla beans 8 tsp vanilla paste8 tsp vanilla extract
300g ground almonds
160g walnuts, finely chopped
70g cake flour
Â½ tsp baking powder
80ml amaretto
Icing
120g 70% chocolate
30g butter
Directions
not sure if this should be baked with fan or not. With fan was fine, but try without next time
Heat oven to 160C. Grease and paper a 9-inch cake pan
Using an electric mixer beat the butter and sugar together until light
Stir in the vanilla
Beat in the eggs, one at a time
Mix the almonds, walnuts, flour, and baking powder together, then fold into the butter mix
Stir in the Amaretto
Bake until set - check after 1 hour. Signs include it coming slightly away from the side of the tin, skewer coming out more or less clean
Last time I took it out after 1 hour with internal temp 107C (which seems impossibly high from what i read on the internet), and few microcrumbs sticking to skewer. Was just about cooked enough. Recipe indicated 1.25 - 1.5 hours
Turn out and leave to cool on a rack
Melt chocolate and butter in bain marie, mix, cool a bit and use as icing
