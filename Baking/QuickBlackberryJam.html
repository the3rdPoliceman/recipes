Quick Blackberry Jam
Ingredients
300g Blackberries (ideally left to thaw completely)
130g granulated sugar
0.33 teaspoons agar agar powder
zest of 1 lemon (optional)
Directions
If the berries are not thawed in advance, put them in a covered saucepan with a little water and steam thaw them over medium low temp
Sieve to separate the juice and berries
Boil the juice and for a few minutes
Add the sugar and the berries and boil for a few more minutes
Taste for sugar
Add the agar agar and boil while stirring for 1 more minute
Store in sterilized jars for up to 2 months
