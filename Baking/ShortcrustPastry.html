Shortcrust Pastry
Ingredients
200g flour
150g butter, from fridge
pinch of salt
Directions
Makes enough for 25cm tart (no lid)
Sift the flour and mix in the salt
Rub in the butter, like for shortbread
Sprinkle in enough water so it forms a dough
Bash into a pastie shape and chill in the fridge for 20 minutes before using
