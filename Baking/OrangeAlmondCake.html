Orange & Almond Cake
cm deep cake
Moro - The Cookbook
Ingredients
Cake
4 eggs, separated
160g caster sugar
150g ground almonds
finely grated zest of 2 oranges
Syrup
juice of 5 oranges, preferably Seville
juice of 1 lemon (if not using Seville oranges)
1 stick cinnamon
50g caster sugar
Directions
Rose and sank pretty severely, leaving it looking like a skateboarding bowl. Maybe less egg white....or more?...internet suggests both. The cake pan seemed overly full in the first place.
Overcooked on top and bottom at 180C - have reduced to 160C for next time
Baking time sees high too - check after 40 minutes
A few flaked almonds or pistachios on top would help appearance
Heat oven to 160C and line the cake pan
Put a tablespoon of the caster sugar in with the egg whites
Beat the rest of the caster sugar with the egg yolks until pale
Mix in the almonds and zest
Beat the egg whites and sugar until stiff and then fold into the egg mixture
Bake for about 50-70 minutes until golden on top and firm to the touch
To make some orange syrup, gently simmer the orange juice, lemon juice, cinnamon and sugar until reduced to 50% of the original weight. Taste for sugar (should be quite tart) and allow to cool
