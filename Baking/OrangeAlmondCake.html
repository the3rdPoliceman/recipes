Orange & Almond Cake
cm deep cake
Cake
4 eggs, separated
160g caster sugar
150g ground almonds
Zest of 2 oranges, finely grated
1g salt
Syrup
juice of 5 oranges, preferably Seville
juice of 1 lemon (if not using Seville oranges)
1 stick cinnamon
50g caster sugar

  1. Toast the almonds
  2. Heat oven to 160C and line the cake pan
  3. Put a tablespoon of the caster sugar in with the egg whites
  4. Beat the rest of the caster sugar with the egg yolks until pale
  5. Mix in the almonds and zest
  6. Beat the egg whites and sugar until stiff and then fold into the egg mixture
  7. Bake for about 50-70 minutes until golden on top and firm to the touch
  8. To make some orange syrup, simmer the orange juice, lemon juice, cinnamon and sugar until reduced to 50% of the original weight. Taste for sugar (should be quite tart) and allow to cool
