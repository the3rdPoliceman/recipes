Pancakes - American style
Ingredients
160 grams Flour
12.5ml Baking Powder
30ml Sugar
4ml Salt
1 Egg
300ml Milk
40-50g Melted Butter
Method
Beat the Egg.  Combine the dry ingredients.  Combine the wet ingredients.  Mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring/folding very gently until just about mixed.  Donâ€™t stress about lumps.  Leave to rest 30 mins.
Cook at medium heat with a little neutral oil.  Use Kitchen paper to spread the oil evenly.  Flip gently when top is nearly dry.
