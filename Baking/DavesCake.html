Dave's Cake (2 layers)
Ingredients
SPONGE
300ml buttermilk (should give 200ml after straining)
3 tablespoons black tea
240g flour
100g ground almonds
2/3 teaspoon salt
2/3 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoon ground cardamom, seeds toasted first if ground at home
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ginger
Zest of 1.5 oranges
Zest of 0.5 lemon
230g unsalted butter, at room temperature
100g fine white sugar
100g fine brown sugar
70g dark brown sugar
3 medium eggs, room temp
2 tsp vanilla extract
FILLING
vanilla something
black tea whatever
Directions
Heat oven to 160C fan. Grease and paper 2 9-inch cake pans
Heat the buttermilk and allow the tea to infuse into it, then allow to cool
If using cardamom pods: Shell, toast and grind the cardamom
Sieve the flour, almonds, salt, baking powder, baking soda, cardamom, cinnamon, ginger together. Add in the zests and whisk it all to combine
With an electric mixer, beat the butter and sugars together on high speed until smooth and creamy
Beat in the eggs & vanilla extract gradually on high speed until combined
Fold in the the dry ingredients
Strain the buttermilk, and top up to 220ml if necessary
With the mixer on low, pour in the buttermilk and mix just until combined
Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cakes pass the toothpick test
Take out of the tin after 5 minutes and cool on a rack
