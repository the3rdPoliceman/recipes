Vanilla Cake (2 layers)
Ingredients
260g flour
40g cornflour
0.75 teaspoon salt
0.75 teaspoon baking powder
0.5 teaspoon baking soda
230g unsalted butter, room temperature
260g granulated sugar
3 medium eggs, room temp
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
240ml buttermilk, at room temperature*
Directions
to 160C fan. Grease and paper 2 24cm cake pans.
Sieve and whisk the flours, salt, baking powder, and baking soda together, and set aside.
With electric mixer, beat the butter and sugar together on high speed until smooth and creamy
Beat in the eggs, white, vanilla extract gradually on high speed until combined.
Fold in the the dry ingredients.
With the mixer on low, pour in the buttermilk and mix just until combined.
Bake for 23-26 minutes or until the cakes are baked through.
To test for doneness, insert a toothpick into the center of the cake.
