Banana Bread
Ingredients
120g butter
150g brown sugar
3-4 ripe bananas
2 eggs 
1Â½ tsp vanilla
180g flour
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt
100g walnuts
Method
Heat oven to 170C
Chop butter, and allow to soften. Beat with the sugar.
Mash the bananas, and beat in the eggs and vanilla. Add to the butter and beat to combine.
Mix flour, baking soda, salt and walnuts. Fold into the mix
Bake in 30cm loaf tin until cocktail stick comes out clean (55-60 minutes)
