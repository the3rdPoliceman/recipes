Julia's Wald Muffins
Julia!
Ingredients
50g ground almonds
270g flour
2/3 teaspoon salt
2/3 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
230g unsalted butter, at room temperature
210g fine brown sugar
30g dark brown sugar
30g honey
3 medium eggs, room temp
1 tsp vanilla extract
Zest of 6 oranges
Zest of 1 lemon
40g grated dark chocolate
220ml buttermilk
Directions
Not sure on cooking time - for whole cake with a similar recipe it was 23-26 minutes
Heat oven to 160C fan
Sieve and whisk the almonds, flour, salt, baking powder, and cinnamon together, and set aside
With electric mixer, beat the butter and sugars together on high speed until smooth and creamy
Beat in the honey, eggs, vanilla extract gradually on high speed until combined
Fold in the zests and chocolate
With the mixer on low, pour in the buttermilk and mix just until combined
Bake until the muffins are cooked through - guessing around 20-25 minutes
To test for doneness, insert a toothpick into the center of the muffins
Decorate with some old dried up herbs to look like a scary forest
