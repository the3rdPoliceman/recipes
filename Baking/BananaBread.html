Banana Bread
120g butter
150g brown sugar
3-4 bananas, riper is better
2 eggs
1Â½ tsp vanilla
180g flour
1 tsp baking soda
3/4 tsp salt
100g walnuts
  1. Heat oven to 170C
  2. Chop butter, and allow to soften. Beat with the sugar
  3. Mash the bananas, and beat in the eggs and vanilla. Add to the butter and beat to combine
  4. Mix flour, baking soda, salt and walnuts. Fold into the mix
  5. Bake in 30cm loaf tin until cocktail stick comes out clean (55-60 minutes)
