Mint Tea
4
Ingredients
2 Tablespoons Chinese Gunpowder Tea
Hot Water
1 large Bunch Mint
4 Tablespoons Sugar
Directions
Wash the teapot and add the tea
Add one cup water and leave without disturbing for a minute
Drain into a cup and keep
Add one cup water and swish around for 30 seconds
Drain and discard
Add reserved cup of tea back into the teapot along with mint and sugar, and fill with hot water
Bring the tea to the boil
Serve, pouring from a height
Put back in pot to mix
Serve again
Second cup (the one you discard) should be darker and dirtier looking than the first
