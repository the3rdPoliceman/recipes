Limoncello
Ingredients
500ml Purest alcohol
Zest of 10 Lemons
Weak Sugar Syrup
500ml water
170g sugar
Strong Sugar Syrup
175ml water
550g Sugar
Directions
ALTERNATIVE: https://gizmodo.com/how-to-make-the-best-limoncello-youve-ever-had-1533528095
SOURCE: italian dude  http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aeYYyY-jIgY
If only waxed lemons are available, lemons should be washed in warm water and scrubbed to remove wax. 
Mix alcohol & lemon zest in jar, and leave somewhere dark for 3 weeks (maybe until peel goes pale?), shaking every 2-3 days
Add weak sugar syrup which will include most of the necessary water, to get it to 50% alcohol
Adjust sweetness with strong sugar syrup
