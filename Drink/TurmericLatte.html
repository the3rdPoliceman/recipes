Turmeric Latte
Ingredients
600ml milk
1 tsp Turmeric
1/8 tsp Ground Ginger
1/2 tsp Cinammon
1/2 tsp Vanilla
1 pinch of salt
2 grinds Black Pepper
honey, to taste
Directions
Still needs some work
Bring everything except the honey to the boil, then simmer for 5 minutes
Add honey to taste
Froth or blend
