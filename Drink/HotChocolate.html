Hot Chocolate (thick)
Ingredients
10g powdered milk
200ml whole milk
10g sugar
60g lindt 70%, finely chopped
0.15g salt
Directions
NOTE: Also try versions with tonka, vanilla, coffee
Whisk the milk into the powdered milk
Whisk in the sugar
Bring the mixture to a boil, then stir in the chopped chocolate and bring to a boil again
