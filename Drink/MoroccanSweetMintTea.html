Mint Tea for 4
Ingredients
2 Tablespoons Chinese Gunpowder Tea
Hot Water
Big Bunch Mint
4 Tablespoons Sugar
Directions
Wash Pot
Add Tea
Add one cup water and leave without disturbing for a minute
Drain into a cup and reserve
Add one cup water and swish around for 30 seconds
Drain and discard
Add reserved cup of tea, big bunch mint, sugar to the pot and fill with hot water
Bring tea to the boil
Serve, pouring from a height
Put back in pot to mix
Serve again
Notes:
Second discarded cup should be darker and dirtier looking than the first
Gunpowder tea looks more like pellets than normal tea
