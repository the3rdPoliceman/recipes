Ishan's Chicken
Ingredients
1Kg Chicken Pieces
100g Full Cream, or Yogurt
4g Extra Hot Chili Powder
5g Garam Masala
14g Salt
3g Black Pepper
1 Small bunch coriander
30g Garlic (about 5 cloves)
15g Root Ginger (Thumb size piece)
Directions
Chop or mince everything finely, mix together and leave overnight
Grill
