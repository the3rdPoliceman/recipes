Hungarian Chicken
2
https://www.chowhound.com/post/dinner-part-liv-740919?commentId=5996744
Ingredients
4 chicken thighs
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/3 large yellow onion, finely sliced
2 ounces mushrooms, sliced about 1/4" thick
2 cups chicken stock
200g Creme Fraiche
2 tsp sweet paprika
1/8tsp chilli flakes
Noodles
Directions
NOTE: Probably worth trying the soy sauce, lemon juice, parsley from the hungarian mushroom soup
NOTE: Can be done with or without chicken skin. If with, the sauce should be poured around the chicken to keep the skin crisp
Heat oven to 175C
Heat oil in casserole on medium and fry chicken until its nicely brown
At the same time, in a frying pan, fry the onions gently for five minutes. Add the mushrooms and give 5 minutes more
Add the stock, creme fraiche, paprika, chilli. Combine and correct salt
If theres a lot of fat in the chicken, remove most of it. Then pour the sauce over the chicken, sprinkle more paprika on top, and bake until chicken
is cooked (about 25 minutes)
Serve with dill on top
