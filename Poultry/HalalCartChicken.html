Halal Cart Chicken
Ingredients
Chicken
3 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon ground coriander
7 cloves of garlic
50ml light olive oil
Salt and black pepper
1 chicken, jointed
Rice
30g butter
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
2 teaspoon cumin seeds
300g rice
600ml chicken broth
Salt and black pepper
Directions
Blend all the marinade ingredients, and marinade the chicken for 1-4hrs
Preheat the oven to 180C, with baking dish inside
Clean the marinade off the chicken (saving it)
Season the chicken, and brown in a frying pan with some oil
Meanwhile, start the rice, by melting the butter, and frying the turmeric, and cumin, and cook for about a minute until fragrant but not browned
Add the rice and fry for a few minutes before adding the stock, and seasoning
Take the baking dish out of the oven, put the rice in it
Cover the chicken in the reserved marinade, and place on the rice along with some more black pepper
Cook in the oven until done
