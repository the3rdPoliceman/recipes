Halal Cart Chicken
Chicken
3 tablespoons lemon juice
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon ground coriander
7 cloves garlic
50ml light olive oil
Salt and black pepper
1 chicken, jointed
Rice
30g butter
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
2 teaspoon cumin seeds
300g rice
600ml chicken broth
Salt and black pepper
  1. Blend all the marinade ingredients, and marinade the chicken for 1-4hrs
  2. Preheat the oven to 180C, with baking dish inside
  3. Clean the marinade off the chicken (saving it)
  4. Season the chicken, and brown in a frying pan with some oil
  5. Meanwhile, start the rice, by melting the butter, and frying the turmeric, and cumin, and cook for about a minute until fragrant but not browned
  6. Add the rice and fry for a few minutes before adding the stock, and seasoning
  7. Take the baking dish out of the oven, put the rice in it
  8. Cover the chicken in the reserved marinade, and place on the rice along with some more black pepper
  9. Cook in the oven until done
