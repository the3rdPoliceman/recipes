Indian BBQ Chicken
Ingredients
Chicken
3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
1 tablespoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon ground coriander seed
10 Cloves garlic
90ml light olive oil
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
1 chicken, jointed
Rice
30g butter
1/2 teaspoon turmeric
2 teaspoon cumin seeds
300g rice
600ml chicken broth
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
Directions
Blend all the marinade ingredients, and marinade the chicken for 1-4hrs
Preheat the oven to 180C, with baking dish inside
Clean the marinade off the chicken (saving it)
Brown the chicken in a frying pan with some oil
Meanwhile, start the rice, by melting the butter, and frying the turmeric, cumin, and about a third of the marinade and cook until fragrant but not browned, about 1 minute
Add the rice and fry for a few minutes before adding the stock, and seasoning
Take the baking dish out of the oven, put the rice in it, and place the chicken on top
Cook until done
