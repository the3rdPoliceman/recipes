Indian BBQ Chicken
Ingredients
1Kg Chicken Pieces
100g Full Cream or Yogurt
5g Cayenne Pepper 
5g Garam Masala 
14g Salt
3g Black Pepper 
Small bunch of Fresh coriander
30g Cloves Garlic (about 5 cloves)
15g Root Ginger (Thumb size piece)
Method
Chop or mince everything finely, mix together and leave overnight.
Grill.
