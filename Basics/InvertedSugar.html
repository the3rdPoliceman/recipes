Inverted Sugar
Ingredients
1kg sugar
480ml water
1g citric acid 1g cream of tartar
Directions
In a non reactive saucepan stir to a boil the sugar, water and citric acid
Once the mixture boils, wash away any sugar crystals stuck to the side of the pan with pastry brush dipped in water
Boil over medium heat, without stirring until it reaches 114C
Remove from heat and let cool
