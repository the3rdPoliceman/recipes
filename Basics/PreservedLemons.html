Preserved Lemons
Ingredients
5 lemons
4 tablespoons salt
150-200ml lemon juice (enough to cover the lemons in the jar)
1 sprig rosemary optional
2 sprigs thyme optional
10 peppercorns optional
1 spicy red pepper optional
Directions
Sterilize a liter Jar
Cut a cross in the lemons about Â¾ of the way through to create a space to stuff with salt
Stuff the slits with the salt, and squash the lemons into the jar
After a week, add enough lemon juice to cover and of the optional flavourings
After 3 more weeks they'll be ready to use
