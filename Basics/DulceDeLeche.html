Dulce de Leche
Ingredients
1 Tin of sweetend condensed milk (or more)
Directions
Put unopened tin on condensed milk in a saucepan, and cover well with water
Be sure there's enough water to cover the tins throughout the whole process, topping up if necessary
Bring to the boil, and simmer for 2.5 hours, plus or minus 1 hour for light or darker caramel
