Crème Pâtissière
Ingredients
250ml of whole milk
1 vanilla pod, or 1 tsp vanilla extract
50g of caster sugar
3 egg yolks
10g of plain flour
10g of cornflour
NOTE: (use 15g and 15g for a very thick Crème Pâtissière)
Directions
Heat the milk and vanilla until almost boiling
Mix the sugar, egg yolks and flours together well
Gradually add the milk to the egg mix, and then pour it all back into the pan
NOTE: In the following step the custard will try to stick, and will go very lumpy – don't worry, the lumps will go
Cook until the mixture thickens, making sure it doesn't go over 85C
