Cream Cheese Icing
Ingredients
75g cream cheese, softened to room temperature (see notes)
20g unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
90g icing sugar
1/2 teaspoon heavy cream or milk
1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Directions
The recipe specified using a brick of philadelphia cheese, which seems quite different to the tub stuff. So this recipe turns out more liquid than it should. Experiment with using Emmi Gala, Migros Budget Frischk√§se or adding some powdered milk to thicken. See further notes
Beat the cream cheese and butter together until smooth
Add the icing sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt, and beat hard for about 3 minutes until completely combined and creamy
Add more Icing sugar if frosting is too thin, or milk if frosting is too thick
Taste for salt
