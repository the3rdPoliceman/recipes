Cream Cheese Icing
Ingredients
75g cream cheese, left to hang overnight in cheesecloth and squeezed
20g unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
90g icing sugar
1/2 teaspoon heavy cream or milk
1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Directions
Next time try cutting sugar to 60g
Try sifting the icing sugar a couple of times, to see if that reduces grittiness
The original recipe specified using a brick of philadelphia cheese but that's not available here. Migros Budget Frischk√§se is better than Philadelphia from the tub. See further notes
Beat the cream cheese and butter together until smooth
Add the icing sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt, and beat hard for about 3 minutes until completely combined and creamy
Add more Icing sugar if frosting is too thin, or milk if frosting is too thick
Taste for salt
