Duck Breast
Ingredients
One Duck Breast at Room Temp
Directions
Remove the skin in as few pieces as possible. Trim all the connective tissue and nasty bits from the Duck Breast. Put skin back in place. Wrap in Cling Film. Cook at 100C until it feels like Medium, Flipping occasionally. Let rest in the cling film, flipping occasionally. Remove from Cling Film, discard the Skin, dry the surface of the meat, season generously and sear in as hot a pan as possible
