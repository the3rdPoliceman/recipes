Meatballs in Tomato Sauce
Ingredients
25g tablespoons breadcrumbs
120ml heavy cream
1 tsp salt
1 tablespoon parsley
3 tablespoons grated parmesan
large pinch of nutmeg
1 egg
1 small onion
200 gram ground beef
300g ground pork
6 tbsp olive oil
1 tin tomatoes
Directions
Tried a similar recipe to this. In this one I reduced the breadcrumbs, and the cream a bit
Try with some other herbs - oregano or rosemary
Mix breadcrumbs, cream, salt, parsley, cheese, and nutmeg, and leave to stand for 10 minutes
Beat egg into cream, followed by onion, then mix into the meat
Beat your meat well, and form into balls
Fry in olive oil until brown
Remove all the oil and fat from the pan, add the tomatoes and some salt
cover and simmer gently for 20 minutes, until the sauce is saucy
