Paletilla de Cordero
1 lamb shoulder (1.5kg), from a young lamb
    Try not slashing meat next time
  1. 1-2 days in advance of cooking, salt the lamb with 1.25% salt by weight of lamb, e.g. 19g salt for 1.5kg lamb shoulder
  2. Leave the lamb out for a couple of hours before cooking to come up to temperature
  3. Put in a roasting dish, with 600ml warm water, slashing the thickest part of the meat a few times about 1cm deep
  4. Start in the oven at 130C (no fan) and cook 45 minutes each side
  5. Raise the temp to 160C and cook 45 minutes each side
  6. Take the lamb out of the oven, and let the temperature heat up to 250C
  7. When the oven is up to temperature, put the lamb back in and cook briefly until the crust is deep brown
