Koftas
Ingredients
500g lamb mince, 30% fat min.
100g pistachios
1 large bunch flat leaf parsley
1 onion
1 clove of garlic
6g salt
Pepper
1/2 tsp chilli powder
Directions
NOTE: Try with mint, coriander, cumin, breadcrumbs
Chop everything finely and add to the lamb mince
Form mini burger shapes, chill to help hold shape and grill
