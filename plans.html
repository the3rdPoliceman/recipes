Lombok Chicken
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w3nAM-2Uk8M
Ingredients: CHICKEN
1 Small chicken (800g)
40 g coconut milk
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp chicken broth powder
A pinch of ground pepper

PASTE INITIAL
5 cloves of garlic
8 shallots
5 g hazelnut
3 red chilies
10 red bird's eye chilies
20 g kencur (galangal is closest)
1 tomato
1 tsp shrimp paste (maybe fried)

PASTE SECOND
1 tsp salt
2 tsp mushroom stock
A pinch of ground pepper

PASTE OTHER
A couple of tablespoons of coconut milk
1 tomato medium

TO SERVE
Lime wedges
Some salads
Rice

Cut the backbone out of the chicken, and crack it along the breastbone to flatten i.e spatchcocked
Massage in the other chicken ingredients
Start the paste by blending all the initial ingredients, along with a teaspoon of oil and enough water so oit blends to a double cream consistency. (Start at a butter chicken color)
Fry the paste for a couple of minutes, then add the other paste additions, and continue cooking until it starts to split and release the oil (should be like a rich, deep ragu red)
Once it darkens a bit, add the coconut milk and cook down, and then the tomato and cook another 5 minutes (Should finish a rich tikka masala color)

Rub the chicken with a bit of oil and grill, turning occasionally, and basting with the paste multiple times. (On the street, they cook it a bit, then dunk it in a bucket of the marinade after it browns a bit and then keep grilling and dunking from there. They all seem to fan the fire too, so it must be cooked at pretty high heat)
Teriyaki Chicken
Ingredients: Sake
Mirin
Soy sauce
Sugar

sushi samurai japan
has good tips

sake 30g
mirin 40g
18g sugar
37g soy
evaporate alcohol a bit from sake and mirin first. then add sugar and soy
Keep it moving so it doesn't burn, and reduce (he says to 1/10 but sceptical)
As its concentration increases, the bubbles get larger. So this can be an indication of reduction
You can test for concentration with a glass of water. See https://youtu.be/y3GSSgwQ6DA?t=248

Makes a teriyaki chicken thigh in a pan with a lot of tips

Dark soy sauce 30ml
Sugar 5g
Mizuame 30g (closest thing is corn syrup or agave syrup)
Water 30ml
Onion 1/4
