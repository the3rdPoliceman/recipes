Ingredients
30g Butter
80g Flour
2 eggs
2g salt
10g sugar
240ml milk
Directions
Try with more salt next time - 3g. Potentially even more is good - bread is 2.4g per 100g flour, or 2.4g per 200g dough and here we have 80g flour and 450g batter
Making the Batter
Melt butter in the frying pan you're going to use to make the crepes
Put dry ingredients, egg, and a bit of the milk in a bowl and whisk to get a smooth thick batter
Add the rest of the milk, and mix it in
Dump the melted butter in to the batter, and mix again
Cooking
Don't add any more butter to the pan - the butter in the mix is enough
Cooking it lower, and spreading the batter thinner results in a significantly crispier crepe
Pour the batter in one side of the pan (rather than the middle) and let it flow across
Thick chocolate sauce - melt 2 parts chocolate to 1 part cream together in a small pan
