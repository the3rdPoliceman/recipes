Cardamom and Orange Panna Cotta
Small Bowls
Ingredients
500g cream
80g sugar
6 cardamom pods
zest of half an orange
2.5 sheets gelatine
Directions
Toast the cardamom pods until fragrant
Heat the cream, sugar, cardamom and orange zest until quite hot and the sugar dissolves. Leave to infuse for 30 minutes
Soak the gelatine in water for 5 minutes
Drain the gelatine, and whisk into the cream mix
Strain into bowls or glasses and refrigerate until set (overnight)
