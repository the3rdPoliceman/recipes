Vanilla ice cream
240ml milk
300ml cream
120g sugar
1.3g salt
1/2 vanilla pod
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla paste
  1. Heat milk, cream, vanilla bean, salt then infuse half an hour
  2. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar
  3. Mix inthe milk, and heat to 80C and a bit thick
  4. Add the vanilla extract, chill & churn
