Vanilla ice cream
Ingredients
240ml milk
300ml cream
120g sugar
1.3g salt
1/2 vanilla pod
4 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla paste
Directions
Heat milk, cream, vanilla bean, salt then infuse half an hour
Whisk the egg yolks and sugar
Mix inthe milk, and heat to 80C and a bit thick
Add the vanilla extract, chill & churn
