Fruit Crumble
4
Ingredients
250g flour
50g ground almonds
pinch of salt
175g sugar (brown, white or mix)
200g butter
fruit, for filling - see below
Directions
Record amount of salt next time
Try some cardamom in the crumble next time when making with raspberry and orange zest
Mix the flour, almonds, salt and sugar
Rub in the butter
Put on top of whatever fruit you're using
Bag of raspberries, zest of one or two oranges, some sugar
Bag of raspberries, few apples, some sugar
