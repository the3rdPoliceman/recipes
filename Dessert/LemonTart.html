Lemon Tart
Pastry
250g plain flour
70g icing sugar
125g unsalted butter, cubed
2 egg yolks
Filling
5 eggs
140g caster sugar
150ml double cream
juice of 2.5 lemons
zest of 3 lemons
Directions
To make the pastry, mix the flour and icing sugar in a bowl
Rub the butter into the flour with your fingers until crumbly
Mix in the egg yolks
If the pastry is still too dry to come together, add 1-2 tbsp water
Flatten out the pastry with your hands, wrap in cling film, and chill for at least 30 mins
While the pastry is chilling, make the filling
Beat the eggs, caster sugar, cream and lemon juice together
Sieve the mixture, then stir in the zest
Roll out the pastry on a lightly floured surface to about the thickness of a Â£1 coin, then fit into a 23cm tart tin
Stab a few holes in the bottom with a fork and put back in the fridge for 30 mins
Heat oven to 160C/140C fan/gas 3
Blind bake for 10 minutes having filled with foil & beans/rice
Remove the foil and beans/rice and bake for another 20 mins until biscuity
Remove it from the oven, pour in the lemon mixture and bake again for 30-35 mins until just set
Leave to cool, then remove the tart from the tin and serve at room temperature or chilled
