Arroz con leche
Small Bowls
200 grams rice
400 grams water
2.6 liters full milk
2 cinnamon sticks
1 vanilla pod
Zest of 1 lemon, in big pieces
Zest of 1/2 orange, in big pieces
100 grams butter
350 grams sugar
4 grams salt
15ml anisrum
    Next time, weigh the milk rice before and after cooking to see how much milk needs to evaporate to give good texture
  1. Add the rice and water to a pan and cook until no liquid is left
  2. Start boiling the milk, cinnamon, vanilla and zest in another pan
  3. Add the boiled milk and stuff to the rice and place on a medium heat
  4. Stir with a wooden spoon regularly until it boils. From here on it must be stirred continuously
  5. When the mixture becomes thick (leaves a trail on the spoon), gradually add the butter
  6. Once the butter is well incorporated, remove the rice from the heat and let the temperature drop slightly
  7. Sprinkle in the salt and 300g of sugar while stirring(whisking is probably too rough)
  8. Taste and see if it needs more sugar. While hot, it should taste a little too sweet
  9. If using alcohol, stir it in now
  10. Leave to cool
Serving Suggestions
  • Sprinkle sugar on top and grill
  • Sprinkle powdered cinnamon on top
