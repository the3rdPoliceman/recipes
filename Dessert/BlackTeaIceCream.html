Black Tea Ice Cream
Ingredients
15g loose black tea (preferably a berry flavor)
250ml milk
500ml full cream
150g sugar
5 egg yolks
Directions
Heat the milk, cream and tea until it's about to boil, leave to steep for an hour
Mix together the egg yolks and sugar, then add the milk mixture through a sieve
Bring the whole mix up to 75-80C
Chill and churn
