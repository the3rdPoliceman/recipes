Candied Oranges
Ingredients
2 oranges, very fresh
300 g white sugar
150 ml Water
1 tbsp baking soda
Directions
Wash oranges well
Dry and cut into thin slices
Put in a saucepan and cover with water. Add the baking soda and bring to a boil
AS SOON as they start to boil, drain and put back in a clean saucepan. (If they boil too much the fruit disintegrates)
Add 150 ml of water and 300 gr of sugar
Cook over low heat for forty minutes, more or less, with the pan covered
Remove and let cool in the pan
Put the slices on a rack to dry
