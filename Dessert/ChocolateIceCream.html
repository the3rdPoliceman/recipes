Chocolate Ice Cream
Ingredients
40g powdered milk
700ml whole milk
65g sugar
200g dark chocolate, finely chopped
Directions
FROM Pierre Herme
ATTEMPT 1: Using half lindt 70% half lindt fleur de sel - tasted great but was quite grainy
ATTEMPT 2: Using lindt 70% - taste was not quite as good, not quite sweet enough (needs 10% more sugar i guess). Texture was less grainy but still a bit
ATTEMPT 3: Using half recipe - 100g lindt 70%, with 20g white sugar and 20g inverted sugar
NOTE: Apparently the graininess is likely caused by cocoa butter clumping together during freezing. A high cocoa mass chocolate like valrhona p125 should help
NOTE: Also try versions with inverted sugar, tonka, vanilla, coffee, salt(start at 0.25g per 100g chocolate)
Gradually whisk the milk into the powdered milk
When the powdered milk is dissolved, whisk in the sugar
Bring the mixture to a boil, then stir in the chopped chocolate and bring to a boil again
Chill and churn
