Chocolate Ice Cream
Pierre Herme
Ingredients
40g powdered milk
700ml whole milk
65g sugar
200g dark chocolate, finely chopped
Directions
1: Using half lindt 70% half lindt fleur de sel - tasted great but was quite grainy
2: Using lindt 70% - taste was not quite as good, not quite sweet enough (needs 10% more sugar i guess). Texture was less grainy but still a bit
3: Using half recipe - 100g lindt 70%, with 20g white sugar and 20g inverted sugar
4: Using half recipe - 350g milk, 100g lindt 70%, 25g light brown sugar, 25g inverted sugar, 0.9g salt, 0.3g xanthan gum, 15ml kahlua, 1/2 tsp vanilla. Very smooth and nice flavor. Not sure the taste is quite as good as attempt 1
Apparently the graininess is likely caused by cocoa butter clumping together during freezing. A high cocoa mass chocolate like valrhona p125 should help
Also try versions with tonka, vanilla
Gradually whisk the milk into the powdered milk
When the powdered milk is dissolved, whisk in the sugar
Bring the mixture to a boil, then stir in the chopped chocolate and bring to a boil again
Chill and churn
