Olive Oil Ice Cream
Ingredients
420ml milk
120 cup heavy cream
1/3 vanilla bean
100g sugar
5 egg yolks
2.5g salt
30ml extra-virgin olive oil
(optional) flaky salt & olive oil to serve
Directions
Put milk, vanilla and 75g sugar into the milk. Boil then leave to infuse for half an hour
Mix the remaining sugar, the egg yolks and the salt in a bowl
Heat up the milk again, then gradually mix into the egg mix
Put this mix back in the saucepan and heat until 80C to custard
Chill, strain, and churn, adding the olive oil half way through churning
