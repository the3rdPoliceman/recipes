Banoffee
Ingredients
Base
225g digestive biscuits
100g pecans
125g butter, melted
Caramel
125g salted butter, diced
100g soft brown, or unrefined raw sugar
400g dulce de leche
Â½ tsp salt
Top
60g pecans
4-5 ripe bananas
Squeeze of lemon juice
275ml double cream
Directions
Crush the biscuits, mix with the pecans and butter, and press into the base of a 23cm pan
For the caramel, heat the butter and sugar until the sugar melts. Mix in the salt and dulce de leche, bring to the boil and whick until smooth. Pour over the base and cool
Slice the bananas, cover with a little lemon, and arrange on the caramel
Whip the cream and put on top
Put the pecans on top (they could also be caramelized with a little sugar)
