Tiramisu
(8 servings = 1 big pyrex dish)
7 egg whites
8 egg yolks
180g Sugar
750g Mascarpone
4 tbsp Grand Marnier
350g Savoiardi Biscuits
270ml strong coffee
Unsweetened cocoa powder
Directions
Coffee amount is approximate - 100ml Coffee to 100g biscuits was definitely too much
I had no Marsala, so used half the amount of Grand Marnier. Kahlua would also be an option
One third recipe for small dish
Make the coffee and cool it completely
Get 3 bowls
Beat the egg whites until stiff in one
Beat the egg yolks and sugar in the next until pale
Beat the mascarpone and marsala (or other alcohol) in the third until softened
Combine the egg yolks and mascarpone, then fold in the egg whites
Dunk the biscuits quickly in the coffee (work out a clever way to get this right)
Layer biscuit, cream, biscuit, cream
Chill until ready to serve, and then top with cocoa powder
