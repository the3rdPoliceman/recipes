Chocolate Mousse
Ingredients
90g dark chocolate
80g butter
20ml espresso
2 eggs, separated
50g sugar
5g dark rum
5g water
pinch of salt
3g vanilla extract
Equipment
Bowl for chocolate bain marie
Bowl for egg yolk bain marie
Bowl for ice water bath for egg yolk bowl
Bowl for whipping egg whites
Method
1. Melt chocolate, butter and coffee in a bain marie
2. Fill a large bowl with ice water and set aside.
3. In another bain marie, whisk the yolks of the eggs with the 50g of sugar, rum, and water for about 3 minutes until the mixture is thick.
4. Remove the bowl from heat and place the bowl in the bowl of ice water. Beat gently until cool and thick, then fold in the chocolate mixture.
5. In another bowl, beat the egg whites with the salt until frothy. Continue to beat until they start to hold their shape. Add a teaspoon of sugar and continue to beat until thick and shiny, but not completely stiff, then add the vanilla.
6. Fold one-third of the beaten egg whites into the chocolate mixture, then the rest
7. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours until firm.
