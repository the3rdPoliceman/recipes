Christmas Pudding
1 liter pudding
70g suet
40g self-raising flour
130g dark brown sugar
110g breadcrumbs
1/2 teaspoons ground mixed spice
1/4 teaspoon grated nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
50g sultanas
60g raisins
110g currants
40g flaked almonds
25g mixed peel
zest of 1/2 orange
zest of 1/2 lemon
juice of 1/3 lemon
2/3 Bramley apples, grated
60g walnuts, chopped
100g treacle
1.3 eggs
40ml brandy
40ml rum
40ml Guinness
    Mix everything in a big bowl. Leave liquid to soak in for a few hours, stirring occasionally
    Fill into pudding bowl, and press down slightly so you don't get gaps
    Seal them for cooking with a lid, or foil with a pleat
    Put in a pan, with a spacer at the bottom (I used vegetable steamer in the pressure cooker) fill water half way up the pudding bowl, and steam for 4 hours
    Feed with brandy every month, resealing with new foil each time
    On the day, cook again the same way, for about 2 hours
