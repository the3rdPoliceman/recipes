Cardamom and Orange Ice Cream
Ingredients
7g green cardamom pods (about 2 tablespoons)
420ml full cream
180ml milk
0.25 teaspoon salt
100g sugar
5 egg yolks
1 tsp vanilla
zest of 1 orange
Directions
Dry fry cardamom for a couple of minutes until it smells good, then crush the pods
Bring milk, cream, salt and cardamom to the boil, then leave to steep for 30 minutes (more and it will et bitter)
Whisk the egg yolks and sugar together, then add the milk mixture
Heat slowly to 75-80C, take off the heat, and add in the zest and vanilla
Chill and churn
