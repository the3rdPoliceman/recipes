Lemon ice cream
Ingredients
150g sugar
10g cornflour
1/8 tsp salt
2 large eggs
juice and zest of 3 lemons
250g full cream
15ml limoncello or gin
Directions
Try 7g cornflour next time
Try 2 lemons next time, for more subtle flavor
Put everything except the cream and limoncello in a saucepan
Heat over medium until it thickens and starts bubbling. Let it bubble for 30 seconds more
Strain it into the cream, then mix, chill and churn
