Sweet Potato Agnolotti
(as starter)
Sweet Potato Filling
1200g sweet potatoes
100g unsalted butter
4 slices bacon
1/4 teaspoon grated nutmeg
Kosher salt and black pepper
Sage Cream
10 large sage leaves, chopped (save the smaller leaves for the fried sage leaf garnish)
200g creme fraiche
200g Buerre Monte (below)
Pinch of Kosher salt (to taste)
Canola oil for deep-frying
48 tiny sage leaves (reserved from above)
25g unsalted butter
4 thin slices prosciutto, cut crosswise into fine julienne
1 recipe Agnolotti
Directions
Oven roast sweet potato
Fry the bacon, and blend it to paste
In a frying pan, mix the bacon, sweet potato flesh, nutmeg and butter. Fry for a while to reduce the moisture
Chill, and then make the agnolotti
For the sauce, make beurre monte, mix in warm creme fraiche, then blend along with Sage
Serve with deep fried sage leaves and shredded ham
