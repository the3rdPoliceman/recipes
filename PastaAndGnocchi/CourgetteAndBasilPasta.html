Courgette and Basil Pasta
Ingredients
½ Courgette
½ Clove Garlic
Olive Oil
Cream
Basil
Parmesan
Directions
Chop Courgette into cigarettes and thinly slice the Garlic. Fry Courgette, with Salt & Pepper. Add the sliced garlic after a couple of minutes, and fry until courgette starts to color
Add splash of Cream to the pan and allow to thicken a bit, then mix in Basil leaves once the sauce looks good
Serve with Parmesan
