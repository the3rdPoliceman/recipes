Basic Agnolotti Dough
Ingredients
200g Flour
130g eggs
large pinch of salt
Normally for 200g flour i'd use 3 eggs yolks and add enough of the whites to get to 130g egg in total. It's flexible though
Directions
Make a well from the flour and put eggs and salt in the middle
Whisk the eggs with a fork, gradually incorporating flour until it's dry enough so you can work it and keep your hands pretty clean
Knead, until smooth
Roll
