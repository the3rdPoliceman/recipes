Salmon and Prawn Agnolotti
Ingredients
Pasta
half recipe of basic agnolotti pasta
Filling
200g salmon
200g prawns
100g ricotta
juice of 1/4 lemon, to taste
dill
black pepper
Sauce
100ml cream
large pinch of saffran
salt & pepper
dill, chopped
Directions
Try with 150g salmon, 50g smoked salmon, 200g prawns next time. Also try some egg yolk to stabilize the filling
Prepare the pasta
Blend the filling ingredients
Roll and stuff the pasta
Make the sauce by steeping the saffran in hot water for a few minutes, then adding to the cream, reducing a bit, and seasoning
Cook the ravioli, and drain before adding to the sauce
Serve with dill on top
