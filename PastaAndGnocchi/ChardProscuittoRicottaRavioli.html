Chard, Prosciutto & Ricotta Ravioli
Ingredients
Pasta
half recipe of basic agnolotti pasta
Filling
250g ChardSpinach
1‚ÅÑ2 small onion, finely chopped
3 slices prosciutto, finely chopped
25g butter
150g ricotta
1 egg yolk
30g grated parmesan
2 pinches grated nutmeg
Sauce
100ml cream
salt & pepper
20g toasted pine nuts
Directions
Prepare the pasta
Sweat the onion and proscuitto in the butter until cooked
Add the spinach, fry and taste for salt
In a bowl, mix the ricotta, egg yolk, parmesan and nutmeg
Let the spinach cool for a few minutes, then mix everything together, and taste for salt & nutmeg
Roll and stuff the pasta
Cook the ravioli, and drain before adding to the sauce
Warm the cream, season and add to the ravioli before serving
