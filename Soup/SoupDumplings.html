Soup Dumplings
Ingredients
120ml water
50g butter
1 teaspoons kosher salt
80g cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
2 large eggs, beaten
chives
Directions
Fill a pot with salted water and boil
Combine the butter, water and salt in a small saucepan and melt over medium heat
Add the ï¬‚our and stir hard until the dough pulls away from the sides
Continue to stir for 4 to 5 minutes without coloring the dough ( A little will stick, that's ok)
When the dough steams a bit and smells nutty, it's done
Put the dough in a bowl, mix in the mustard and mix for a few seconds
Gradually mix in the egg, then the chives
Form the dumplings, and boil until they float
