Soup Dumplings
Ingredients
120ml water
50g butter
1 teaspoons kosher salt
80g cup all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
2 large eggs, beaten
chives
Directions
Fill a wide deep pot with salted water and bring to a simmer
Combine the water, butter, and most of the salt in a small saucepan and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat
Reduce the heat to medium add the ﬂour all at once, and stir rapidly with a stiff heatproof or wooden spoon until the dough pulls away from the sides
Continue to stir for 4 to 5 minutes without coloring the dough ( A little will stick, that's ok)
When you start to notice a little steam rising from the dough and a faint nutty aroma of cooked ﬂour, you’re done
Put the saucepan in water to cool, mix in the mustard and the remaining salt and mix for a few seconds
Gradually mix in the egg, then the chives
Form the dumplings, and boil until they float
