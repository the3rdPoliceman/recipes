Soup Dumplings
Ingredients
120ml water
50g butter
3/4 teaspoon salt
80g flour
1 teaspoon mustard
2 eggs, beaten
chives
Directions
Fill a pot with salted water and boil
Put the butter, water and salt in a small saucepan and melt over medium heat
Add the ï¬‚our and stir until the dough steams a bit and starts to smell nutty, but without coloring - about 4-5 minutes
Take off the heat and mix in the mustard
Gradually beat in the egg, then the chives
Form the dumplings, and cook in the boiling water for about 5 minutes (open one to see if its done)
