Light Celeriac Soup
Ingredients
1 Celeriac
1 Small Onion
1 Garlic Clove
Stock
Cream
Milk
Crispy Bacon to garnish
Directions
Fry Onion and Garlic until soft. Add Celeriac and fry for a few minutes. Add equal parts Stock, Milk and Cream to cover the Celeriac and cook until soft
Blend the soup at high speed for a few minutes until it gets light and slightly foamy. Reheat and serve with the Bacon.
