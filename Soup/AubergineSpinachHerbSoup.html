Aubergine, Herb & Spinach Soup
Ingredients
3 aubergines
3 tbsp lemon juice
Salt and black pepper
100ml olive oil
2 onions, finely chopped
6 cloves garlic
60g coriander leaves, chopped
60g parsley leaves, chopped
40g dill leaves, chopped
5 spring onions, finely sliced
2.5 tsp ground cinnamon
2.5 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp ground turmeric
400g baby spinach, chopped
500ml vegetable stock
1 red chilli, finely chopped
2 tsp black mustard seeds
Directions
Bake the aubergines in a hot oven until charred and soft(maybe an hour?)
Take the flesh out and mix with a third of a teaspoon of salt, a tablespoon lemon juice and a good bit of pepper
Fry the onion in 60ml of oil until deep brown. Add the garlic and fry 30 seconds more
Turn the heat down to medium low, and add most of the chopped herbs, spring onions, and 50ml water. Cook for 15 minutes
Turn up the heat again, add the spices and fry for 30 seconds, then add the spinach, stock and 400ml water, 1.75 teaspoons salt and black pepper
Blitz 300ml of the soup, and mix back in
In a separate pan, cook the chilli in 40ml oil slowly fo about 10 minutes, then add the mustard seeds and cook until they pop, and add a touch of tumeric
To serve, divide the aubergine between the bowls, add the soup, extra chopped herbs and drizzle with the chilli oil
