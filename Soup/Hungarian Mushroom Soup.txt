Hungarian Mushroom Soup
Ingredients
2 onions
300g mushrooms
1 bunch of dill
1 tablespoon paprika
1 tablespoon soy sauce
600ml stock
30g butter
3 tablespoons flour
300ml milk
1 teaspoon salt
Black Pepper
2 teaspoons lemon juice
150ml sour cream
1 bunch of parsley
Method
Slice and fry the onions, add mushrooms, 1 tsp dill, 1/2 cup stock, soy sauce, and paprika.
Cover and simmer 15 minutes.
Make a roux with the butter, flour and milk.
Stir the remaining stock and mushroom mix into the roux.
Cover and simmer 10-15 minutes.
Just before serving, add salt, pepper, lemon juice, sour cream, parsley and the rest of the dill.
