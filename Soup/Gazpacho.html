Gazpacho
Ingredients
1kg tomatoes
1/2 onion, roughly chopped
1 red pepper, seeded and chopped
1 green pepper, seeded and chopped
1 garlic clove
1/4 cucumber, chopped
1 slice of bread
200ml cold water or ice
100-150ml olive oil (more gives a creamier gazpacho)
60ml sherry vinegar
1tsp salt
Directions
It's important to keep everything cold. So best start with everything from fridge, and possibly most of the water as ice
Blend everything well until smooth - maybe hold back a bit of vinegar
Taste for salt and vinegar
Ice, croutons, diced cucumber, peppers, onion, drizzled olive oil can be added as toppings when serving
