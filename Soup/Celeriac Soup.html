Celeriac Soup
Ingredients
Butter
1 Celeriac
1 Potato
1 Leek
1 Onion
1 Clove Garlic
Stock
Crispy Pancetta Garnish
Directions
Roughly chop vegetables and slice the garlic. Add to pan with the butter and fry until they soften.
Add the stock, and simmer until the celeriac is completely tender. Liquidise.
