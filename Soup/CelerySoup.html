Celery Soup
1 head of celery, including leaves
1 large potato
1 onion
100g butter
750ml chicken stock (maybe vegetable is better)
1 bunch of dill
150ml heavy cream
Directions
Dice onions, celery and potatoes and cook in butter for 15 minutes, along with some salt
Add the stock and cook for 10 more minutes
Add dill, and a handful of the leaves and blend
Stir in the cream, and season
