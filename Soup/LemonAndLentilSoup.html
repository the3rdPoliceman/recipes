Brocolli, Cheese & White Wine Soup
Ingredients
butter
Small onion chopped
head of broccoli
300 ml chicken stock
300 ml dry white wine
2 tsp cornflour
150 ml milk
225 g Blue Cheese
Directions
Fry onion in butter
When it softens, add the brocolli, fry for a few minutes, then add the stock and wine
Cover and simmer brocolli is cooked
Mix the cornflour with the milk and stir it into the soup
Bring to the boil and simmer gently for 2-3 minutes
Melt in the cheese
