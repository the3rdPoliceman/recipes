Lemon and Lentil Soup
Ingredients
200g green lentils
80ml olive oil
2 large onions
5 garlic cloves
2 tbsp cumin seeds
2 lemons
Salt and black pepper
3 medium waxy potatoes
400g chard (leaves and stalks separated) or spinach
1 litre chicken/vegetable stock
1Â½ tbsp coriander leaves
2 spring onions
Directions
Cover lentils in lightly salted cold water, bring to boil and cook until reasonably soft (20-25 minutes)
Add oil onions, garlic, cumin, 2tsp salt, lots of pepper, and the peel from one of the lemons(use peeler to get large pieces) to another pan, and fry until golden
Add in the potatoes, lentils, stock, and chard stems and simmer until potato is done
Add spinach for a couple of minutes
Add lemon juice, and some spring onion
Add coriander and remaining spring onion as garnish
