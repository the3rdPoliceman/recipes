Brocolli, Cheese & White Wine Soup
butter
1 Small onion chopped
1 head of broccoli
300 ml chicken stock
300 ml dry white wine
2 tsp cornflour
150 ml milk
225 g Blue Cheese
  1. Fry onion in butter
  2. When it softens, add the brocolli, fry for a few minutes, then add the stock and wine
  3. Cover and simmer brocolli is cooked
  4. Mix the cornflour with the milk and stir it into the soup
  5. Bring to the boil and simmer gently for 2-3 minutes
  6. Melt in half the cheese
  7. Pur√©e the soup and season
  8. Serve with the rest of the cheese on top
