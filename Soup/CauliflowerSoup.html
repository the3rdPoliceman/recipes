Cauliflower Soup
Ingredients
50g butter
1 cauliflower
1 leek
1 onion
1/2 tsp curry powder
Salt
300ml milk
200ml water
350ml heavy cream
Directions
Dice the onion, leek and cauliflower, and add to a deep pan with butter, curry powder and some salt. Fry gently, covered with baking paper until the vegetables are almost tender
Add the milk, cream, and water bring to a boil
Simmer gently for 30 minutes
Puree in the blender/kitchen machine until smooth and foamy
Return to the pan and add check for salt (normally it needs quite a lot)
