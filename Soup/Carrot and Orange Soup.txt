Carrot and Orange Soup
Ingredients
600g Carrots
2 Small Onions
200g Potatoes
Spices
Cream
1 Orange
Stock
Method
Roast Carrots and Onions.  Add to saucepan with Potatoes and Stock.  Bring to the boil and cook until Potatoes are done.  Blend and add Spices, Orange Juice to taste.  Add some Cream & Season.
Ideas
1 	Serve with Sour Cream, mixed with very finely sliced or grated Orange Zest
2	Fresh Ginger
