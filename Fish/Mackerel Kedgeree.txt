Mackerel Kedgeree
Ingredients
150g basmati or brown rice
1 onion, sliced
3 tsp mustard seeds
1.5 tsp turmeric powder
1.5 tsp chilli powder
150g Spinach
100g smoked mackerel
2 eggs
Method
Start to cook the rice. 
Sliced onion and fry on low until soft
Start eggs boiling for 7 minutes.
Increase the heat, and add the mustard seeds to the frying pan. When they start to pop, add the chilli powder and turmeric. Fry for a minute. Add the spinach and flake in the mackerel, and fry until cooked. Add the rice once it is ready.
