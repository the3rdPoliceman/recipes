Salt Cod Pie
300g Salted Cod
1 Liter fish stock
300ml Milk
2 Bay Leaves
20ml Olive Oil
2 medium Onion, chopped
1.5 cloves garlic, crushed
10g Plain Flour
25g Butter
450g Mashed Potato
1 tbsp Parsley, chopped
nutmeg, to taste
little bit of parmesan for the top
This is a total work in progress
  1. Cook the potatoes in fish stock, mash with salt, pepper, nutmeg. Reserve
  2. Divide the cod into chunks. Add half the chopped garlic and parsley and mix, then cover with hot milk and let rest for 30 minutes to fluff up well.
  3. Heat the olive oil, sliced onion, remaining garlic and bay leaves, and saute until the onion is translucent and soft. Reserve.
  4. Remove cod from milk and squeeze gently to remove excess liquid. Reserve milk for the bechamel.
  5. Make Bechamel using butter, flour and milk.
  6. Butter Caserole, then add onion mix, fish, bechamel, potatoes, cheese and give 20 minutes at 200C
