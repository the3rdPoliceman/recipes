Ahi Poke
Ingredients
340g raw sashimi-grade tuna, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 very small shallot thinly sliced
1 scallion, thinly sliced
1 teaspoon white or black sesame seeds, or a mix
20ml soy sauce, more or less to taste
10ml toasted sesame oil, more or less to taste
5ml honey, more or less to taste
Crushed red pepper, to taste (optional)
Kosher salt
Sushi rice
Directions
Cook sushi rice and cool.
Mix everything else together 5 minutes before serving, and serve on the rice.
