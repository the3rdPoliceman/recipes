Grilled Peaches with Amaretto & Mint
30g almonds, coarsly chopped
25g butter
50g sugar
4 ripe peaches, halved if possible or cut in large chunks
50ml amaretto
Zest of 1/2 lemon
Small bunch of mint leaves of 1/2 lemon
  1. Place the butter and half the sugar in a pan over medium to high. When the sugar begins to melt, add the peaches skin-side down and cook until the bottoms begin to brown (about 5 minutes)
  2. Add the grand marnier, flame it, and the remaining sugar and continue caramelizing for 1 more minute
  3. Turn the peaches over to brown the flesh sides (about 1 minute)
  4. Finely grate over the lemon zest, and add the mint leaves
