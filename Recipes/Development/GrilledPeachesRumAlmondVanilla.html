Grilled Peaches with Rum, Almond, Vanilla
30g almonds, coarsly chopped
50g butter
100g sugar
4 ripe peaches, halved if possible or cut in large chunks
60ml vanilla rum
Zest of 1/2 lemon
  1. Lightly toast the almonds in a pan over medium heat
  2. Turn up the heat a bit and place the butter and half the sugar in the pan. When the sugar begins to melt, add the peaches skin-side down and cook until the bottoms begin to brown (about 5 minutes)
  3. Add the rum, flame it, and the remaining sugar and continue caramelizing for 1 more minute
  4. Turn the peaches over to brown the flesh sides (about 1 minute) and add in most of the almonds
  5. Finely grate over the lemon zest, and remaining almonds as you serve
