Swedish Meatballs
1 onion
1 tbsp butter
3 tablespoons bread crumbs
200ml heavy cream
1 egg
1 tsp salt
Pinch of cinnamon
large pinch of nutmeg
large pinch of ground cloves
200 gram ground beef
300g ground pork
1 tbsp oil
1 tbsp butter
lingonberry jam cranberry jam
1/4 recipe quick pickled cucumbers
Original meat was 200g beef, 100g pork, 100g veal, but this was what i could find

  1. Finely chop the onion and fry slowly in the butter until well caramelized
  2. Mix 150ml of the cream and bredcrumbs and set aside for at least 10 minutes
  3. Mix the egg, salt, spices and onion into the cream. Then add this all to the meat
  4. Beat the meat vigorously for a couple of minutes until it's really well mixed, then form into walnut sized meatballs (water on hands makes this easier)
  5. Fry in the onion pan with a little oil if needed, adding a little butter towards the end
  6. When the meatballs are done, take them out and add the rest of the cream to the pan to make a sauce
  7. Serve with the berries, cucumber, and mashed potato
