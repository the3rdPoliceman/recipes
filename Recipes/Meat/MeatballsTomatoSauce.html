Meatballs in Tomato Sauce
60ml full cream
1 egg
30g tablespoons breadcrumbs
1 tsp salt
1 tablespoon parsley, chopped
large pinch of nutmeg
1 small onion
30g grated parmesan
200 gram ground beef
300g ground pork
6 tbsp olive oil
1 tin tomatoes
  1. Beat the cream and egg together, then add the breadcrumbs, salt, parsley and nutmeg, and leave to stand for 10 minutes
  2. Mix the onion and grated parmesan into the meat, the stir in the cream mixture
  3. Beat your meat well, and form into balls
  4. Fry in olive oil until brown
  5. Remove all the oil and fat from the pan, add the tomatoes and some salt
  6. cover and simmer gently for 20 minutes, until the sauce is saucy
Notes
  • Tried a similar recipe to this. In this one I reduced the breadcrumbs, and the cream a bit
  • Try with some other herbs - oregano or rosemary
