Lemon & Vanilla Cake
cm cake
180g flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/3 teaspoon baking soda
170g unsalted butter, at room temperature
180g white sugar
2 medium eggs, room temp
2 tsp vanilla extract
150ml buttermilk
Zest of 3 lemons
40g chopped dark chocolate
40g chopped white chocolate
  1. Heat oven to 160C fan
  2. Sieve and flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda together, and set aside
  3. With electric mixer, beat the butter and sugar together on high speed until smooth and creamy
  4. Beat in the eggs and vanilla extract gradually on high speed until combined
  5. With the mixer on low, pour in the buttermilk and mix just until combined
  6. Fold in the zests and chocolate
  7. Bake until the cake is cooked through - around 35 minutes
  8. To test for doneness, insert a toothpick into the center of the cake, or listen
