Julia's Wald Muffins
50g ground almonds
270g flour
2/3 teaspoon salt
2/3 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
230g unsalted butter, at room temperature
210g fine brown sugar
30g dark brown sugar
30g honey
3 medium eggs, room temp
1 tsp vanilla extract
Zest of 6 oranges
Zest of 1 lemon
40g grated dark chocolate
220ml buttermilk
  1. Heat oven to 160C fan
  2. Sieve and whisk the almonds, flour, salt, baking powder, and cinnamon together, and set aside
  3. With electric mixer, beat the butter and sugars together on high speed until smooth and creamy
  4. Beat in the honey, eggs, vanilla extract gradually on high speed until combined
  5. Fold in the zests and chocolate
  6. With the mixer on low, pour in the buttermilk and mix just until combined
  7. Bake until the muffins are cooked through - probably around 18-25 minutes
  8. To test for doneness, insert a toothpick into the center of the muffins
  9. Decorate with some old dried up herbs to look like a scary forest
