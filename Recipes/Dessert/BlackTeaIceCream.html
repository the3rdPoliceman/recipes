Black Tea Ice Cream
15g black tea (preferably a berry flavor)
250ml milk
500ml full cream
150g sugar
5 egg yolks
  1. Heat the milk, cream and tea until it's about to boil, leave to steep for an hour
  2. Mix together the egg yolks and sugar, then add the milk mixture through a sieve
  3. Bring the whole mix up to 75-80C
  4. Chill and churn
