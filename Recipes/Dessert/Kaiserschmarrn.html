Kaiserschmarrn
250ml Milk
1 Pinch Salt
10g White Sugar
125g Flour
4 Eggs (separated)
25g melted Butter
  1. Beat the egg whites to stiff peaks with the sugar and the pinch of salt
  2. Combine the yolks and milk, then beat together
  3. Beat in the flour until smooth
  4. Carefully fold the egg whites into the batter to keep the mixture light
  5. Heat a very large frying pan over medium and add half the butter, then pour in the batter and cook for about a minute
  6. Transfer to a preheated 180C oven and bake until cooked through
  7. Brush the top with melted butter, then flip and cook on the stovetop until browned on the second side
  8. Chop up the pancake, and serve with whatever you like
Serving Suggestions
  • Serve with powdered sugar, apple sauce, or fruit compote
