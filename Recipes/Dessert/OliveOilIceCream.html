Olive Oil Ice Cream
420ml milk
120ml heavy cream
1/3 vanilla bean
75g sugar
25g inverted sugar
5 egg yolks
2.5g salt
30ml extra-virgin olive oil
olive oil & flaky salt to serve
try 300ml full cream, 250ml milk
try 3 g salt next time
try 40ml oil next time
  1. Bring milk, cream, vanilla, inverted sugar and 50g sugar to the boil, then leave to infuse for half an hour
  2. Mix the remaining sugar, the egg yolks and the salt in a bowl
  3. Heat up the milk again, then gradually mix into the egg mix
  4. Put this mix back in the saucepan and heat until 80C to custard
  5. Chill, strain, and churn, adding the olive oil and beating to break up ice crystals half way through churning
