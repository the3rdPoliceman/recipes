Lemon Tart
Pastry
250g plain flour
70g icing sugar
125g unsalted butter, cubed
2 egg yolks
Filling
5 eggs
140g caster sugar
150ml double cream
juice of 2.5 lemons
zest of 3 lemons
  1. To make the pastry, mix the flour and icing sugar in a bowl
  2. Rub the butter into the flour with your fingers until crumbly
  3. Mix in the egg yolks
  4. If the pastry is still too dry to come together, add 1-2 tbsp water
  5. Flatten out the pastry with your hands, wrap in cling film, and chill for at least 30 mins
  6. While the pastry is chilling, make the filling
  7. Beat the eggs, caster sugar, cream and lemon juice together
  8. Sieve the mixture, then stir in the zest
  9. Roll out the pastry on a lightly floured surface to about the thickness of a Â£1 coin, then fit into a 23cm tart tin
  10. Stab a few holes in the bottom with a fork and put back in the fridge for 30 mins
  11. Heat oven to 160C/140C fan/gas 3
  12. Blind bake for 10 minutes having filled with foil & beans/rice
  13. Remove the foil and beans/rice and bake for another 20 mins until biscuity
  14. Remove it from the oven, pour in the lemon mixture and bake again for 30-35 mins until just set
  15. Leave to cool, then remove the tart from the tin and serve at room temperature or chilled
