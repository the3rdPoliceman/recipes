Chocolate Lava Cakes
Ganache
40g dark chocolate
10g butter
60ml cream
5ml coffee extract
Cake
85g dark chocolate
35g butter
2.5 egg, separated
90g sugar
40g cornflour
40g ground almonds
0.5g salt
    Preparing the Ganache
  1. Make the ganache by heating the cream and melting the other ingredients into it
  2. Freeze, and cut into cylinders 3cm long, and 4cm smaller in diameter than your ring molds
    3. Making the Cakes
  3. Melt the chocolate and butter
  4. Whisk the egg white until stiff, add half the sugar and continue whisking until shiny
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolk with the other half of the sugar until pale
  6. Fold the chocolate and butter into the egg yolk mixture, then gently fold in a third of the egg whites, the remaining egg whites, and finally the cornflour, almonds, and salt
    7. Assembling and Baking
  7. Butter the ring molds, line with baking paper, and then butter the paper
  8. Place a sheet of baking paper on a baking tray, and put the ring molds on top
  9. Fill the ring mold half full of cake mix, prod the cylinder of ganache into the middle, and top with more cake mix until 80% full
  10. Bake at 180C for 15 minutes
  11. Find a de-ring-molding tool - this should be something a bit narrower and taller than the ring mold, and will help you get the cake out af the mold e.g. a tall narrow glass or a tea strainer
  12. For each cake, slide a palette knife under the ring mold, then lift the ring mold and cake and set on top of your de-ring-molder. Slide the ring mold down off the cake, put your cake on it's final plate before removing the paper
