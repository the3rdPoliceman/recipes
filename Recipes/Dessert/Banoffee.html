Banoffee
Base
225g digestive biscuits
100g pecans
125g butter, melted
Caramel
125g salted butter, diced
100g soft brown unrefined raw sugar
400g dulce de leche
Â½ tsp salt
Top
60g pecans
4-5 bananas, ripe
Squeeze of lemon juice
275ml double cream
  1. Crush the biscuits, mix with the pecans and butter, and press into the base of a 23cm pan
  2. For the caramel, heat the butter and sugar until the sugar melts. Mix in the salt and dulce de leche, bring to the boil and whisk until smooth. Pour over the base and cool
  3. Slice the bananas, cover with a little lemon, and arrange on the caramel
  4. Whip the cream and put on top
  5. Put the pecans on top (they could also be caramelized with a little sugar)
