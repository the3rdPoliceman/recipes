Cardamom and Orange ice cream
7g green cardamom pods (about 2 tablespoons)
420ml full cream
180ml milk
0.25 teaspoon salt
100g sugar
5 egg yolks
1 tsp vanilla
zest of 1 orange
  1. Dry fry cardamom for a couple of minutes until it smells good, then crush the pods
  2. Bring milk, cream, salt and cardamom to the boil, then leave to steep for 30 minutes (more and it will get bitter)
  3. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar together, then add the milk mixture
  4. Heat slowly to 75-80C, take off the heat, and add in the zest and vanilla
  5. Chill and churn
