Cafe Latte Ice Cream
350ml milk
4 tbsp coffee powder (ground to number 7 mittel in migros machine)
260g double cream
30g invert sugar
100g brown granulated sugar
4 egg yolks
35ml kahlua
1.5tsp vanilla paste
In Development. Could be stronger coffee flavor, less sweet, less vanilla. Next time try 6tbsp coffee, 80g brown sugar, 35 ml kahlua, 1 tsp vanilla
  1. Bring the milk to boil, add the coffee, then cool and rest for 6 hours in fridge. Strain out the coffee with fine strainer. (yields around 290g after filtering out grounds)
  2. Add the cream, invert sugar, brown sugar and egg yolks then heat to 80C (doesn't necessarily go very custardy).
  3. Chill, mix in the kahlua and vanilla, then churn.
