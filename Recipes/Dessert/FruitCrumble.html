Fruit Crumble
This basic crumble recipe will work for almost any type of fruit. Variations to the crumble mix, such as different nuts, sugar etc. are also possible
250g flour
50g ground almonds
pinch of salt
175g sugar (brown, white or mix)
200g butter
fruit, for filling - see variations
  1. Mix the flour, almonds, salt and sugar
  2. Rub in the butter
  3. Put on top of whatever fruit you're using
Notes
  • Record amount of salt next time
  • Try some cardamom in the crumble next time when making with raspberry and orange zest
Variations
  • Bag of raspberries, zest of one or two oranges, some sugar
  • Bag of raspberries, few apples, some sugar
  • Not tried this idea: 500g rhubarb, zest of an orange and a squeeze of juice, some form of vanilla, few tablespoons sugar
