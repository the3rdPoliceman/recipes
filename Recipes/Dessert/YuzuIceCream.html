Yuzu ice cream
225g sugar
15g cornflour
1g salt
3 eggs
200ml Yuzu Juice
370g full cream
  1. Put everything except the cream in a saucepan
  2. Heat over medium until it thickens and starts bubbling
  3. Strain it into the cream, then mix, chill and churn
