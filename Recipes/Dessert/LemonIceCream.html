Lemon ice cream
150g sugar
2 tsp cornflour
1/8 tsp salt
2 large eggs
3 lemons
250g full cream
15ml limoncello gin
  1. Zest and juice the lemons, and put everything except the cream and limoncello in a saucepan
  2. Heat over medium until it thickens and starts bubbling. Let it bubble for another 30 seconds
  3. Strain it into the cream and limoncello, then mix, chill and churn
Notes
  • 3 Lemons is extremely lemony, less would also be ok.
Variations
  • Use 2 lemons and 120g sugar
  • Use 1 lemons and 100g sugar, for a much more subtle flavor
