Lemon ice cream
150g sugar
10g cornflour
1/8 tsp salt
2 large eggs
juice of 3 lemons
zest of 3 lemons
250g full cream
15ml limoncello gin
Try 7g cornflour next time
Try 2 lemons next time, for more subtle flavor
  1. Put everything except the cream and limoncello in a saucepan
  2. Heat over medium until it thickens and starts bubbling. Let it bubble for 30 seconds more
  3. Strain it into the cream, then mix, chill and churn
