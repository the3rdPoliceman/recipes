Lemon & Basil Ice Cream
2 tsp cornflour
2 eggs
1.5 lemons
2g salt
250g full cream
15ml limoncello
100g sugar
25g basil leaves
  1. Zest and juice the lemons, then put the cornflour, eggs, lemon juice, zest and salt in a saucepan
  2. Warm over medium heat until it thickens and starts bubbling
  3. Remove from the heat, and pour in the cream and limoncello
  4. Using a mortar and pestle, pulverise the basil and sugar until it becomes a smooth green goop (alternitively use a food processor). Add the basil goop to the ice cream base, and stir well to combine
  5. Taste for salt, then chill overnight
  6. Strain through a sieve to remove the lemon zest, and churn
