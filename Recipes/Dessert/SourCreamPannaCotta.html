Sour Cream Panna Cotta
Small Bowls
1.6 sheets gelatine
400g sour cream creme fraiche
60g buttermilk
65g sugar
1 tsp vanilla paste vanilla essense
  1. Soak the gelatine in water for 5 minutes
  2. Heat the sour cream, sugar and buttermilk until quite hot and the sugar dissolves
  3. Drain the gelatine, add to the sour cream mix along with the vanilla, and whisk until incorporated
  4. Strain into bowls or glasses and refrigerate until set (overnight)
