Cardamom and Orange Panna Cotta
Small Bowls
500g cream
85g sugar
6 cardamom pods
zest of half an orange
2.5 sheets gelatine
  1. Toast the cardamom pods until fragrant
  2. Heat the cream, sugar, cardamom and orange zest until quite hot and the sugar dissolves. Leave to infuse for 30 minutes
  3. Soak the gelatine in water for 5 minutes
  4. Warm the cream mix, drain the gelatine, and whisk into the cream
  5. Strain into bowls or glasses and refrigerate until set (overnight)
