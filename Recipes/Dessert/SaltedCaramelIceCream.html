Salted Caramel Ice Cream
300ml full cream
150g Jacutinga sugar (unrefined sugar/vollrohrzucker)
1Â½ teaspoons of salt
200ml milk
With a high end machine, 150ml cream, 150ml milk & 200ml water should be possible, giving more intense caramel flavour
  1. Boil the cream
  2. In a frying pan, melt the brown sugar over a medium heat until completely melted (mine burned here and there, which seemed to be good)
  3. Add the warmed cream and salt, remove from the heat and whisk
  4. Add the milk (and water if using), return to the heat until it reaches 80C
  5. Remove from heat, chill, churn
