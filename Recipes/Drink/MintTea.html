Mint Tea
2 Tablespoons Chinese Gunpowder Tea
Hot Water
1 large bunch of Mint
4 Tablespoons Sugar
  1. Wash the teapot and add the tea
  2. Add one cup water and leave without disturbing for a minute
  3. Drain into a cup and keep
  4. Add one cup water and swish around for 30 seconds
  5. Drain and discard
  6. Add reserved cup of tea back into the teapot along with mint and sugar, and fill with hot water
  7. Bring the tea to the boil
  8. Serve, pouring from a height
  9. Put back in pot to mix
  10. Serve again
    11. Second cup (the one you discard) should be darker and dirtier looking than the first
