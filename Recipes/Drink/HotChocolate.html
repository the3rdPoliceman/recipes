Hot Chocolate
180ml milk
30ml cream
pinch salt
tonka
2tsp raw brown sugar
30g 78% lindt
10ml vanilla rum
  1. Heat the milk with the cream, salt and tonka until near boiling
  2. Add in the chocolate and heat until it boils, and starts foaming up the saucepan. Allow it to foam briefly to get some bubbles into the hot chocolate
