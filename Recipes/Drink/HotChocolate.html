Hot Chocolate (thick)
Cup
10g powdered milk
200ml whole milk
10g brown sugar
40g lindt 70%, finely chopped 30g lindt 78%
0.15g salt
Few orange peelings (credit Noelia Gonzalez Lopez)
1 grating tonka beans
2 tsp rum vanilla extract
Also try versions with cardamom, coffee, raw sugar
  1. Gradually whisk the milk into the powdered milk and sugar
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then stir in the chopped chocolate and bring to a boil again
  3. If using any of the optional ingredients, the orange peel and tonka would be added while heating the milk, the vanilla would be added just before serving
