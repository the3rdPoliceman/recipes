Hot Chocolate
180ml milk
pinch salt
tonka
2tsp raw brown sugar
30ml cream
30g 78% lindt
10ml vanilla rum
  1. Heat the milk with the salt, tonka, and the sugar until near boiling
  2. Chop the chocolate, add to the milk and heat until it boils and starts foaming up the saucepan. Allow it to foam briefly to get some bubbles into the hot chocolate
  3. Mix in the vanilla and cream and serve
