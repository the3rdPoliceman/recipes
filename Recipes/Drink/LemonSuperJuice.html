Lemon Super Juice
25g Lemon zest
25g citric acid
400g water
Juice from the lemons
  1. Blitz the lemon zest and citric acid briefly in a blender, and leave to sit for 2 hours
  2. Add the water and the juice from the lemons
  3. Blend again very thoroughly, then pass through a sieve
Notes
  • Typically, I'll get 12.5g of zest from one lemon
  • Will keep in a fridge for a couple of weeks
