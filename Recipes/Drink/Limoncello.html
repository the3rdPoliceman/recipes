Limoncello
500ml Purest alcohol
Zest of 10 Lemons
Weak Sugar Syrup
500ml water
170g sugar
Strong Sugar Syrup
175ml water
550g Sugar
Alternative recipe from Gizmodo
  1. If only waxed lemons are available, lemons should be washed in warm water and scrubbed to remove wax.
  2. Mix alcohol & lemon zest in jar, and leave somewhere dark for 3 weeks (maybe until peel goes pale?), shaking every 2-3 days
  3. Add weak sugar syrup which will include most of the necessary water, to get it to 50% alcohol
  4. Adjust sweetness with strong sugar syrup
