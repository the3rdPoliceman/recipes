Coffee Cake
Cake
125g walnuts
125g hazelnuts
150g butter
200g brown sugar
2 eggs
1 egg yolk
100g Plain flour
1 tsp Baking powder
3g salt
1tbsp instant coffee, dissolved in as little hot water as possible
30ml vanilla rum
100ml oil
Icing
160g icing sugar
80g butter
1 teaspoon instant coffee
1 tsp vanilla rum
    Cake
  1. Roast the nuts, then chop the walnuts, and grind the hazelnuts into powder
  2. Cream the butter and sugar, stir in the nuts, and gradually beat in the eggs
  3. Combine the flour, baking powder and salt, and fold into the mix
  4. Combine the coffee vanilla and oil, and fold into the mix
  5. Bake at 160C until done
    6. Icing
  6. Dissolve the coffee powder in the vanilla
  7. Beat the butter well until soft
  8. Seve the icing sugar, and gradually beat into the butter
  9. Finally, beat in the coffee/vanilla mix
Variations
  • Use coffee flavored french buttercream for icing
