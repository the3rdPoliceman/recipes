Chocolate Cake (Kladdkaka Swedish Cake)
135g butter
55g cocoa powder
350g sugar
110g plain flour
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3 eggs lightly whisked
  1. Heat oven to 180C (non fan)
  2. Melt butter, mix in everything except the eggs, mix in the eggs
  3. Paper and butter a cake tin (20-25cm), put the mix in and cook for 20-25 minutes. The top should be firm but crack under a little pressure from your finger
  4. Cool in the pan and remove
