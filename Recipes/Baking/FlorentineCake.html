Florentine Cake
cm round cake
Cake
50g butter
100g fine light brown sugar
2 large eggs
90g plain flour
25g ground almonds
6g baking powder
2g salt
50g buttermilk
25g oil
1 vanilla pod
Topping
65g butter
80g light brown sugar
1.5g salt (not more)
35ml cream
100g flaked almonds
1 tsp of vanilla rum
    Cake batter and first bake
  1. Brown the butter and let it cool
  2. Whisk the eggs with the sugar until pale and doubled in volume
  3. Combine the flour, ground almonds, baking powder and salt, and mix
  4. Whisk the buttermilk, oil, vanilla and melted butter together
  5. Slowly mix the flour and buttermilk mixes into the batter
  6. Bake at 160C until done (25 minutes)
    7. Perpare the topping
  7. While the cake is baking, place the butter, sugar, and salt in a heavy bottom pan and heat for a couple of minutes until the sugar is melter. It doesn't really need to darken in color.
  8. Add the cream, almonds and vanilla and stir well
    9. Complete the bake
  9. Once the cake is done, take it out and turn the oven up to 190C
  10. Add the topping and bake until the topping is darkened to a medium brown (6-8 minutes)
Notes
  • This is a variation on swedish toscakaka
  • Last time was too dense, so I upped the baking powder but haven't tried it since then
Variations
  • Apple Florentine - Chop up 1 to 1.5 finely cut apples and add them to the cake batter before baking
  • Chocolate Florentine - Chop up 100g of dark chocolate and add to the batter before baking
