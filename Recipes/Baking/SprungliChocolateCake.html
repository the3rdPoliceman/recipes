Sprungli Chocolate Cake
by cm cake
CAKE
30g butter
88g egg white (about 4)
100g sugar (1)
140g egg yolk (about 6)
40g sugar (2)
32g white flour
22g cocoa powder
40g ground hazelnuts
2g salt
GANACHE
400g chocolate
400g full cream
1.5g salt
    Making the Cake
  1. Melt the butter in a pan and let it cool
  2. Wash a bowl and whisk really well, wiping with some vinegar on kitchen paper to remove any possible grease. Whisk the egg white together with sugar (1) to stiff peaks
  3. Beat sugar (2) and yolk for about 10 minutes
  4. Mix white flour, cocoa powder, nuts, and salt together
  5. Sieve the dry ingredients onto the egg yolk mixture and fold in carefully, followed by the egg whites and finally the liquid butter
  6. Butter your mould, pour in the batter, and bake at 170C for 30 minutes
  7. Remove from form, cool on a rack. If you want to leave it overnight, cover it with foil, but make sure you let it cool first
    8. Making the Ganache
  8. Warm the cream until it is boiling. Take the pan off the heat, add in the chopped chocolate and salt, and mix until ganache forms
  9. Mix with immersion blender to emulsify well
  10. Place in a shallow bowl, covered with food film, at room temperature. Leave it alone
    11. Assembling
  11. Trim 1.5mm from each side and end of the cake
  12. Discard the top crust, and split remaining cake into 3 layers
  13. Put ganache into piping bag
  14. Reassemble in the tin it was baked in, first lining it with cling film or acetate so it can be easily removed
  15. Spread a thin layer of ganache on the side/ends and a fairly thin layer on the base
  16. Then take a layer of cake, apply ganache to the edges generously and add to the tin
  17. Repeat until done
  18. Then spread all remaining ganache on top, level off
  19. Allow to cool and harden, remove from form
  20. Fix any imperfections with some heated knife
  21. Dust the top with cocoa
