Outrageous Brownies
cm pan
90g Butter
90g Semi-Sweet Chocolate
35g Bakers Chocolate
60g Whole Eggs
2.7g Instant Espresso Powder
5.2g Vanilla Extract
90g Sucrose (Sugar)
30g AP Flour (All-Purpose Flour)
3g Baking Powder
1.2g Sea Salt
70g Chocolate Chips
75g Walnuts
  1. In a pot, melt the butter, chocolate chips, and bakers chocolate
  2. In a bowl, stir (not whisk) together eggs, espresso powder, vanilla, and sugar
  3. Stir the warm chocolate mixture into the egg mixture
  4. Grease a parchment-lined 18cm round pan
  5. Sift 25g flour, the baking powder, and the sea salt together.
  6. Use the remaining 5g flour to coat the chocolate chips and walnuts (this stops them sinking)
  7. Fold these dry ingredients into the wet mixture in 3 additions
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared pan
  9. Bake at 150C for 20 mins, then bang the pan to stop an air bubble forming between the brownies and the pan. Then bake around 15 minutes more, or until a toothpick comes out clean
  10. Allow to cool before cutting
Notes
  • Be careful not to overmix the batter to maintain the fudgy texture.
Adapted via Kathleen Meisinger from Ina Garten's Outrageous Brownie recipe.
