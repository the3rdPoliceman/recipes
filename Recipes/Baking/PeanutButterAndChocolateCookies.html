Peanut Butter & Chocolate Cookies
120g butter
120g peanut butter
250g fine brown sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
150g flour
3g salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
100g dark chocolate, chopped
  1. Beat the butter and peanut butter together until well mixed
  2. Add in the sugar and beat again
  3. Add egg and vanilla...and beat again until well combined
  4. Mix the flour, salt, baking powder and soda. Then add them to the batter and stir to combine
  5. Refrigerate for 30 minutes
  6. Divide into 15 and roll into balls, then place the balls on baking sheets lined with parchment (12 will fit on a big sheet). They will spread a lot so leave space.
  7. Bake at 170C (no fan) for 14 minutes (they shouldn't brown)
Notes
  • These puff up when cooking, but then flatten as they cool and end up as big chewy discs
