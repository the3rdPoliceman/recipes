Mandarin, Almond & Chocolate Cake
cm cake
60g 90% chocolate, frozen
125g unsalted butter, softened
60g fine brown sugar
60g dark brown sugar
3 eggs, separated
1 tsp vanilla extract
100g whole almonds
1 teaspoon ground cardamom
0.33 teaspoon salt
0.25 teaspoon ground cinnamon
zest of 3 mandarins (if skins are very thin, the entire peel can be dried for a day and blitzed)
zest of 1.5 oranges
  1. Heat oven to 150C with fan. Grease and paper your pan
  2. Beat the butter and sugar together until light
  3. Beat in the vanilla and egg yolks, one at a time
  4. Blitz the almonds and frozen chocolate until they're like very coarse sand
  5. Mix the almonds, chocolate, and all the remaining ingredients well together
  6. Fold these into the butter mixture
  7. Beat the egg whites until stiff, and fold into the mix
  8. Bake until done. Check after around 40 minutes for 18cm cake
Notes
  • Try toasting the almonds first
  • Try a version of this without chocolate and with tea used to infuse the butter
Adapted from River Cafe Capri Torte
