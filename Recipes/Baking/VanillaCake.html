Vanilla Cake
cm cakes
260g flour
40g cornflour
0.75 teaspoon salt
0.75 teaspoon baking powder
0.5 teaspoon baking soda
230g unsalted butter, room temperature
260g granulated sugar
3 medium eggs, room temp
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
240ml buttermilk, room temp 240ml milk and 15ml lemon juice, shaken together
Best eaten quickly, as it tends to go hard
  1. Heat oven to 160C fan. Grease and paper cake pans.
  2. Sieve and whisk the flours, salt, baking powder, and baking soda together, and set aside.
  3. With electric mixer, beat the butter and sugar together on high speed until smooth and creamy
  4. Beat in the eggs, vanilla extract gradually on high speed until combined.
  5. Fold in the dry ingredients.
  6. With the mixer on low, pour in the buttermilk and mix just until combined.
  7. Bake for 23-26 minutes (for standard recipe in 23cm pans) or until the cakes are baked through.
  8. To test for doneness, insert a toothpick into the center of the cake.
