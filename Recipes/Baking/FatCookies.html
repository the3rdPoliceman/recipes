Fat Cookies
100g walnuts
150g unsalted butter
230g light brown sugar
20g raw sugar
1 eggs
1 egg yolk
200g dark chocolate
250g cake flour
3g salt
1tsp cornflour
4g baking soda
  1. Toast the walnuts until they smell good and start to brown, then chop them up
  2. Melt the butter and whisk it into the sugar and honey
  3. Beat the egg and yolk together, then slowly whisk into the butter and sugar
  4. Chop up the chocolate, and mix into the flour, salt, cornflour, baking soda and nuts
  5. Add the dry ingredients into the wet, and mix by hand until combined
  6. Chill in the fridge, then form into 100g balls, and bake at 210C for 10 minutes or until the top is brown (don't squish the balls before cooking)
