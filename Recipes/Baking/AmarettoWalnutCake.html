Amaretto & Walnut Cake
Cake
300g butter, softened
300g fine sugar
4 large eggs
2.5 vanilla beans 8 tsp vanilla paste 8 tsp vanilla extract
300g ground almonds
160g walnuts, finely chopped
70g cake flour
Â½ tsp baking powder
80ml amaretto
Icing
120g 70% chocolate
30g butter
not sure if this should be baked with fan or not. With fan was fine, but try without next time
  1. Heat oven to 160C. Grease and paper a 9-inch cake pan
  2. Using an electric mixer beat the butter and sugar together until light
  3. Stir in the vanilla
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time
  5. Mix the almonds, walnuts, flour, and baking powder together, then fold into the butter mix
  6. Stir in the Amaretto
  7. Bake until set - check after 1 hour. Signs include it coming slightly away from the side of the tin, skewer coming out more or less clean
    8. Last time I took it out after 1 hour with internal temp 107C (which seems impossibly high from what i read on the internet), and few microcrumbs sticking to skewer. Was just about cooked enough. Recipe indicated 1.25 - 1.5 hours
  8. Turn out and leave to cool on a rack
  9. Melt chocolate and butter in bain marie, mix, cool a bit and use as icing
