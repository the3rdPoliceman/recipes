Light Shortbread
180g butter
2g salt
90g sugar
1 egg yolk
10ml vanilla extract
200g flour
  1. Cream the butter with the sugar and salt
  2. Beat in the egg yolk and vanilla
  3. Beat in the flour until just combined
Notes
  • Try without egg yolk, and some cornflour
Variations
  • Add the grated zest of an orange or lemon
