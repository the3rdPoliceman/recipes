Carrot Cake
cm deep cake
50g chopped walnuts
60g jacutinga sugar
60g light brown sugar
40g granulated sugar
40g honey
100ml peanut oil
2 eggs
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
140g all-purpose flour
1 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2g teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon ground cardamom
2 pinch ground nutmeg
2 pinch ground cloves
150g grated carrots
60g grated apple
Zest of 1 orange
1 recipe Cream Cheese Icing
Probably can still take a bit more salt. Try 2.5g next time
Tried 30ml Grand Marnier, but didn't notice much effect. Maybe 50ml
Try with brown butter icing from Grandbaby Cakes
  1. Toast the walnuts in the oven, then set the oven to 160C with no fan for the cake
  2. Whisk the sugars, honey, oil, eggs and vanilla together in a large bowl
  3. In a second large bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and cloves together
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and fold the ingredients together until just combined
  5. Fold in the carrots, apple and nuts
  6. Bake in a lined tin until cooked - around 45 minutes for a deep small cake
  7. Cool, do the icing, put the zest on top
