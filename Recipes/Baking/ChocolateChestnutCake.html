Chocolate Chestnut Cake
cm round cake
100g chestnuts (cooked)
20g vanilla rum
140g butter
140g dark chocolate
5 eggs, separated
120g sugar
1g salt
2g vinegar
100g chestnut paste
25g flour
200ml cream
20g Chocolate (for garnish)
  1. Chop the whole chestnuts into pea sized pieces, and soak in the vanilla rum
  2. Heat oven to 180C, no fan
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan, take off the heat and melt the chocolate into it, stirring to combine
  4. Whisk the egg yolks, 80g sugar, and salt until pale and thick.
  5. Whisk egg whites and vinegar until fluffy. Add the remaining sugar and beat until stiff peaks form
  6. Combine the egg yolk mix, chocolate mix, chestnut paste, chopped chestnuts and flour. Fold in the egg whites, and scrape into a lined 23cm pan
  7. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, then cool on a rack
  8. Decorate with whipped cream, and shave chocolate over
Notes
  • 80g Chestnut flour can be used instead of the chestnut puree and flour. A bit extra sugar would be needed too
  • Next time try a bit more salt, only chestnut paste (200g), and another 20% chocolate
