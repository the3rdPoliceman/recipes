Chocolate Chestnut Cake
cm round cake
140g butter
140g dark chocolate
5 eggs, separated
140g sugar
1g salt
2g vinegar
200g chestnuts (cooked)
200ml cream
20g Chocolate (for garnish)
  1. Heat oven to 180C no fan
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan, take off the heat and melt the chocolate into it. Stir together
  3. Whisk the egg yolks, 80g sugar, and salt until pale and thick. Stir into the warm chocolate
  4. Whisk egg whites until fluffy. Add remaining sugar and beat until stiff peaks
  5. Process half the chestnuts into fine powder, and half into pea sized pieces and add to the chocolate batter. Gradually fold in the egg whites, and scrape into a lined 23cm pan
  6. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, cool on a rack
  7. Decorate with whipped cream, and shave chocolate over
Notes
  • 80g Chestnut flour can be used instead of the 100g of pulverised chestnuts. Chestnut flour is expensive (10chf/300g) but cheaper than chestnuts (4chf/100g). It's popular in poland (called MÄ…ka kasztanowa) so maybe googling can find a much supplier cheaper (price in poland - 10eur/kg)
  • Next time try soaking the roughly chopped chestnuts in vanilla run to soften them up a bit
