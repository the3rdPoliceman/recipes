Dave's Cake (2 layers)
cm cakes
SPONGE
300ml buttermilk (should give 220ml after straining)
3 tablespoons black tea
2 teaspoon ground cardamom
240g flour
100g ground almonds
2/3 teaspoon salt
2/3 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
Zest of 2 oranges
Zest of 0.5 lemon
230g unsalted butter, at room temperature
100g fine white sugar
100g fine brown sugar
70g dark brown sugar
3 medium eggs, room temp
2 tsp vanilla extract
FILLING
vanilla something?
SERVE WITH
Cardamom & Orange Ice Cream
  1. Heat oven to 160C fan. Grease and paper cake pans
    2. In the next step, try adding a little cream to the buttermilk to stop it curdling
  2. Heat the buttermilk and allow the tea to infuse into it, then allow to cool
  3. If using cardamom pods: Shell, toast and grind the cardamom
  4. Sieve the flour, almonds, salt, baking powder, baking soda, cardamom, cinnamon, ginger together. Add in the zests and whisk it all to combine
  5. With an electric mixer, beat the butter and sugars together on high speed until smooth and creamy
  6. Beat in the eggs & vanilla extract gradually on high speed until combined
  7. Fold in the dry ingredients
  8. Strain the tea out of the buttermilk, and top up to 220ml if necessary
  9. With the mixer on low, pour in the buttermilk and mix just until combined
  10. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cakes pass the toothpick test
  11. Take out of the tin after 5 minutes and cool on a rack
