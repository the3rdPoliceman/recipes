Peanut Butter Blondies
cm round pan
3 eggs
250g white granulated sugar caster sugar
Pinch of salt
160g peanut butter, chunky
60g butter
60g plain flour
0.25tsp baking powder
60g dark chocolate, cut into chunks
    Batter Preparation
  1. Gently melt the butter and peanut butter together and mix until smooth.
  2. In a separate large bowl, beat the eggs, sugar, and salt until thick and moussy
  3. Mix the flour and baking powder
  4. Pour the melted peanut butter and butter mixture into the beaten eggs.
  5. Add a third of the flour and fold it in, bringing the peanut butter mixture from the bottom and the flour from the top. Repeat two more times until the mixture is evenly combined.
    6. Baking
  6. Pour the mixture into a lined 23cm round pan
  7. If using chocolate, scatter the chunks over the top
  8. Bake in the middle of a preheated oven at 175ºC, with fan, until middle reaches around 100C. Took mine 22 minutes.
    9. Cooling and Serving
  9. Allow the blondies to cool before cutting, and store in an airtight container - they will become fudgier over time.
