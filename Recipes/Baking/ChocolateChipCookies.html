Chocolate Chip Cookies
250g Butter
150g White Sugar
300g Brown Sugar, moist kind. (sub 270g dry, brown 25g treacle)
450g flour
7.5g Baking soda
6g Sea salt
75g eggs
25g egg yolks
200g chocolate chips
  1. Melt butter slowly
  2. Sift flour, salt and baking soda together
  3. Paddle butter with sugars until combined
  4. Paddle at speed 1, add dry ingredients and eggs/yolks in a few stages, alternating between dry and wet. Starting and ending with dry ingredients
  5. Add chocolate chips, paddle until just combined
  6. Divide into 100g lumps
  7. Chill and freeze
  8. Bake from frozen at 160C
