Pistachio & Lemon Cake
cm round cake
cake
150g soft butter
125g granulated sugar
2.5 eggs
40g white flour
1tsp baking powder
60g ground pistachios
100g ground almonds
Zest of 2 lemons
1.5g salt
syrup
Juice of 1.5 lemons
40g granulated sugar
60 g pistachios, roughly chopped
  1. Beat the butter and sugar until pale and light - about 10 minutes
  2. Beat the eggs in a small bowl, and gradually beat into the butter and sugar
  3. Combine the flour, baking powder, ground pistachios, ground almonds, lemon zest and salt in a bowl, then fold into the butter mix, stirring as little as possible
  4. Put the dough in a lined 18cm pan and bake until it starts coming away from the sides and a stick comes out clean. About 35 minutes
  5. Meanwhile, heat the lemon juice and sugar until the sugar dissolves. Add the chopped pistachios, bring to a boil and take off the heat
  6. Remove the cake from the tin, and take the baking paper off the bottom but leave it on the sides. Pour the hot pistachio syrup over the top and let everything cool down completely, then remove the baking paper from the sides
Serving Suggestions
  • Serve with gsreek yogurt
