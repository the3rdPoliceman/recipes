Cream Cheese Icing
75g cream cheese, left to hang overnight in cheesecloth and squeezed
20g unsalted butter, softened to room temperature
90g icing sugar
1/2 teaspoon heavy cream or milk
1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Next time try cutting sugar to 60g
Try sifting the icing sugar a couple of times, to see if that reduces grittiness

  1. Beat the cream cheese and butter together until smooth
  2. Add the icing sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt, and beat hard for about 3 minutes until completely combined and creamy
  3. Add more Icing sugar if frosting is too thin, or milk if frosting is too thick
  4. Taste for salt
