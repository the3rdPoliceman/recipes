Cream Cheese Crème Pâtissière
250ml whole milk
1 vanilla pod 2 tsp vanilla extract
50g caster sugar
3 egg yolks
10g plain flour
10g cornflour
50g cream cheese
  1. Heat the milk and vanilla until almost boiling
  2. Mix the sugar, egg yolks and flours together well
  3. Gradually add the milk to the egg mix, and then pour it all back into the pan
    4. In the following step the custard will try to stick, and will go very lumpy – don't worry, the lumps will go
  4. Cook until the mixture thickens, making sure it doesn't go over 85C
  5. Take off the heat and beat in the cream cheese
Notes
  • Could potentially add more cream cheese if you wanted
