Fried Sage Leaves
Oil
Sage Leaves
Salt
The timing of when to take the leaves out is critical. If allowed to brown, they will be bitter
  1. Heat at least 1.5cm of oil to 185C. Using a saucepan with a long handle will make the next steps easier
  2. Get a sieve ready above another empty saucepan, so you can drain leaves quickly as soon as they are ready
  3. Dump the leaves into the oil
  4. Initially, they will bubble furiously but that will quickly die down. When the bubbles die down almost completely (probably after 20-30 seconds), drain through the sieve, empty onto kitchen paper and add a bit of salt
