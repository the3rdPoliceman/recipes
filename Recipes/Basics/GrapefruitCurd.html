Grapefruit Curd
5 egg yolks(add 2 extra if you want very thick curd)
90g granulated sugar
juice of 1 grapefruit
90g butter
didn't thicken last time. try again one more time with same recipe
  1. Whisk together the eggs, sugar, and lemon juice in the bowl
  2. Place over a saucepan of simmering water and cook, and stir until it thickens and hits 74C
  3. Remove from heat, pour through a sieve to remove lumps
  4. Whisk in the butter
  5. The lemon curd will thicken as it cools
