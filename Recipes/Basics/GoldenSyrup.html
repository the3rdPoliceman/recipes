Golden Syrup
800g Sugar
300g water
25ml lemon juice
  1. Put the water and sugar in a pan and bring to the boil
  2. Add the lemon juice, and mix well
  3. Transfer to a clean pan (which helps to avoid crystals forming) and boil very gently until amber brown. How long this takes depends on how much it boils, but will normally be 30 minutes to an hour
Notes
  • The golden syrup will thicken as it cools
