Quick Blackberry Jam
300g Blackberries (ideally left to thaw completely)
130g granulated sugar
0.33 teaspoons agar agar powder
zest of 1 lemon
  1. If the berries are not thawed in advance, put them in a covered saucepan with a little water and steam thaw them over medium low temp
  2. Sieve to separate the juice and berries
  3. Boil the juice and for a few minutes
  4. Add the sugar and the berries and boil for a few more minutes
  5. Taste for sugar
  6. Add the agar agar and boil while stirring for 1 more minute
  7. Store in sterilized jars for up to 2 months
