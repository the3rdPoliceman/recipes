Lemon Curd
5 medium egg yolks(add 2 extra if you want very thick curd)
130g granulated sugar
juice of 3 lemons
90g butter
  1. Whisk together the eggs, sugar, and lemon juice in a bowl
  2. Place over a saucepan of simmering water and cook, and stir until it thickens and hits 74C
  3. Remove from heat, pour through a sieve to remove lumps
  4. Whisk in the butter
  5. The lemon curd will thicken as it cools
Notes
  • Try with more butter, to see if it's more... buttery
