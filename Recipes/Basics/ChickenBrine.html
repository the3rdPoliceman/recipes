Chicken Brine
1l water, ideally 700ml should be chilled or ice
50g salt
30g sugar
1 bay leaf
4 cloves garlic
1/4 onion
2tsp black peppercorns
1/2 lemon
small bunch Parsley Stalks
Small bunch tarragon
  1. Put 300ml of water in a pot with the rest of the ingredients and heat to dissolve the salt and sugar
  2. Take off the heat, add the rest of the water or ice
  3. Chill further in the fridge if necessary to bring the temperature down to around 5C
