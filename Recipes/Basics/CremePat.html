Crème Pâtissière
250ml whole milk
1 vanilla pod 2 tsp vanilla extract
50g caster sugar
3 egg yolks
10g plain flour
10g cornflour
(use 15g and 15g for a very thick Crème Pâtissière)
20g butter
  1. Heat the milk and vanilla until almost boiling
  2. Mix the sugar, egg yolks and flours together well
  3. Gradually add the milk to the egg mix, and then pour it all back into the pan
  4. Cook while stirring until the mixture thickens, goes lumpy, and looks like a total disaster. Continue cooking for another 30 seconds while whisking hard
  5. If using the butter, take the pan off the heat and whisk it in
Notes
  • This is much less likely to curdle like standard custard, so you can get away with cooking it at higer heat. It can even get away with boiling a bit
  • It using vanilla essence, add it at the end rather than at the start
