Crepes
crepes
30g Butter
80g Flour
2 eggs
2g salt
10g sugar
240ml milk
Try with more salt next time - 3g. Potentially even more is good - bread is 2.4g per 100g flour, or 2.4g per 200g dough and here we have 80g flour and 450g batter
    Making the Batter
  1. Melt butter in the frying pan you're going to use to make the crepes
  2. Put dry ingredients, egg, and a bit of the milk in a bowl and whisk to get a smooth thick batter
  3. Add the rest of the milk, and mix it in
  4. Dump the melted butter in to the batter, and mix again
    5. Cooking
  5. Don't add any more butter to the pan - the butter in the mix is enough
  6. Cooking it lower, and spreading the batter thinner results in a significantly crispier crepe
  7. Pour the batter in one side of the pan (rather than the middle) and let it flow across
Serving Suggestions
  • Thick chocolate sauce - melt 2 parts chocolate to 1 part cream together in a small pan
