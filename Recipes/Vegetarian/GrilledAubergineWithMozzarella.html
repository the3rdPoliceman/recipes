Grilled Aubergine With Mozzarella
3 aubergines
100g mozzarella
olive oil
flaky salt
2 slices bread
20g parmesan (optional)
  1. Preheat a large grill pan to medium high
  2. Slice the aubergines into 4-5mm slices
  3. Sprinkle the grill with salt and olive oil, then place the aubergine slices on top
  4. Grill until well browned on the first side, then srikle with a bit of oil and flip
  5. Rip the bread into chunks and fry on the grill with a bit more oil
  6. Once the aubergine is cooked, grate the parmesan on top, and then take off the grill
  7. Slice the mozzarella and grill until it starts to brown. Take if off and add to the aubergines
  8. One the bread chunks have browned, take off and add to the aubergines
  9. Toss a little together
