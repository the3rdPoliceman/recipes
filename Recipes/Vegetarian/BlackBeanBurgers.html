Black Bean Burgers
burgers
750g cooked black beans (from tin, or 350g dried)
1 onion
3 cloves garlic
60ml olive oil
2 chipotle chillis in adobo
40g pistachios, roasted and roughly chopped
100g feta
15ml worcester sauce
60g breadcrumbs
Zest of 1 lime
1 egg
chilli powder
salt
4 burger buns
Condiments
100g chipotle mayo
150g feta, sliced
1 avocado, with salt and lime juice
1 bunch coriander, finely chopped
  1. Bake the beans at 200C for about 20 minutes until somewhat dehydrated
  2. Meanwhile, fry the onion and garlic in the oil until softened
  3. Blitz around a third of the beans into mush, and break up the rest a bit with a fork
  4. Finely chop the chipotle chillies and mix into the beans, along with the pistachios, feta, worcester sauce, breadcrumbs, lime zest, and the onions, garlic and oil from earlier
  5. Mix in the egg, and then add salt and chilli powder to taste
  6. Form into burgers, and fry over medium to low heat until a thick brown crust forms (be careful turning them, as they tend to fall apart)
  7. Heat the buns if wanted, and serve with the condiments
Notes
  • Try with finely chopped pieces of lime flesh as a condiment to give bursts of lime
