Aubergine Grilled Cheese Sandwich
2 aubergines
200g provolone fontina, gruyere
1 tomato
sprig of basil
salt and pepper
2 eggs
100g breadcrumbs
vegetable oil
  1. Heat the oven to 180C
  2. Cut the aubergines into 6-7mm thick slices, then slice the cheese and tomato
  3. Form the aubergine, cheese, basil leaves and tomato into "sandwiches" with the aubergine taking the part of the bread. Season them with salt and pepper, and squish them a bit
  4. Beat the eggs, brush on both sides and edges of the aubergine and dip the sandwiches in the breadcrumbs
  5. Shallow fry in around half a centimeter of oil over high heat until golden brown on both sides, then place on a baking tray lined with baking paper and bake for another 7-10 minutes
