Aubergine and Spinach Curry
600g aubergines
3 tbsp olive oil
2 onions
Zest of 1/2 lemon
2-3 cloves of garlic
1 tsp garam masala
1 tsp chilli powder
1 tsp turmeric
1 tbsp cumin seeds
2 tsp ground coriander
1 tin chopped tomatoes
400ml coconut milk
250g frozen spinach
1 Small bunch of coriander
  1. Cover the aubergines in 2 tablespoons oil, salt them and roast at 210C until browned
  2. Slice the onions and fry until soft. Add the lemon, garlic and spices and fry a bit more
  3. If using the lemon, Add a little water and boil for a few minutes
  4. Add the tomatoes and conconut milk and simmer
  5. Add the aubergine whenever it's done, and the spinach near the end
