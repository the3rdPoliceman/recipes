Pea Risotto
200g peas
Prosciutto
Mint Oil
  1. Cook the risotto as normal
  2. While the risotto is cooking, cook the peas in salted water and drain
  3. Blitz half the peas along with 50g butter to a puree
  4. When the risotto is done, beat in the parmesan, pea puree and whole peas
  5. As a garnish, add some finely sliced ham, or mint oil
