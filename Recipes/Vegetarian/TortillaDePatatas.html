Tortilla de Patatas
1350g Potatoes
150g onion
25g Salt
10 eggs
Olive Oil
A good non stick pan is essential
  1. Peel the potatoes and use a knife to lever off chunks of the potato rather than chop them
  2. Chop the onion into 1cm dice and put in a bowl with the potatoes and salt
  3. Leave for 20 minutes so water comes out of the potatoes, then drain (maybe dry the potatoes and onion also?)
  4. Heat enough olive oil to fry the potatoes. It should be hot enough that th oil bubbles when a piece of potato is put in, but doesn't go crazy
  5. Add the potatoes and onion and cook until potatoes are tender and onion soft
  6. Turn up the heat a bit at the end to give th potatoes a slight golden edge (15-20 mins total)
  7. Crack three or four eggs into a bowl, mix the drained potatoes into it (a bit of oil does no harm), and stir well until it goes creamy as the potatoes cook the eggs
  8. Put in the rest fo the eggs and stir some more
  9. To cook the final tortilla, get a pan very hot and add a few tablespoons of the cooking oil from before
  10. Add the mix, and flip around like an omlette, until it's gets close to cooked enough. The let settle in the pan so the bottom cooks solid
  11. Drip some oil over the top, flip and cook the other side, then serve
