Spinach Shakshuka
1 Onion
Olive Oil
2 cloves garlic
1tsp mustard seeds
2tsp cumin seeds
0.25tsp chilli powder
400g Spinach 400g Chard
frozen is fine
2tsp coriander powder
4 eggs
50g feta cheese
  1. Fry the onion and garlic in some olive oil until golden brown
  2. Add the mustard seeds and fry until they start popping, then add the cumin seeds and chilli powder and fry for another 30 seconds
  3. Add the spinach, fry until wilted and cooked through and taste for salt
  4. Sprinkle the ground coriander over the spinach and mix in well
  5. Make 4 holes in the spinach, and crack an egg into each. If using feta, scatter it over the top at this point.
  6. Cover with a lid and cook until the eggs are done to your liking
Variations
  • Instead of the spices and feta, add some ham and scatter cubes of fontina on top when you add the eggs
