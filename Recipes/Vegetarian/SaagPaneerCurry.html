Saag Paneer
vegetable oil
300g Paneer
400g spinach
1 medium white onion, sliced
1 thumb ginger, grated
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp turmeric
1 tbsp cumin seed
1 tbsp mustard seeds
1/2 tsp cayenne
1/2 tsp garam masala
2 teaspoons ground coriander
150ml full cream
  1. Dice the paneer and simmer in salted water to soften
  2. Add the onions, garlic, ginger and fry with oil, until golden
  3. add the spices and toast withh a bit more oil until aromatic
  4. Add the spinach and warm, adding enough cream to make it currylike
  5. Add the drained paneer
