Poke
340g raw sashimi-grade tuna, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 very small shallot, thinly sliced
1 scallion, thinly sliced
1 teaspoon sesame seeds (white or black)
20ml soy sauce, more or less to taste
10ml toasted sesame oil, more or less to taste
5ml honey, to taste
Crushed red pepper, to taste
Kosher salt
Sushi rice
  1. Cook sushi rice and cool
  2. Mix everything else together 5 minutes before serving, and serve on the rice
