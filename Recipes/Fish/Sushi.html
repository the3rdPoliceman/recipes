DASHI RECIPE
Ingredients:
1 liter water
10 * 12cm kombu
30g bonito flakes

Method:
Soak the kombu in the water overnight
Heat the water and kombu gently to 75C and then remove the kombu
Continue heating the water until it almost boils (it MUST NOT boil), and remove from the heat
Add the Bonito and stir, leave for five minutes and then strain through cheesecloth to leave the dashi
