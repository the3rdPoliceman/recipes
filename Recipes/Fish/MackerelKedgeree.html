Mackerel Kedgeree
150g basmati rice 150g brown rice
1 onion, sliced
3 tsp mustard seeds
1.5 tsp turmeric powder
1.5 tsp chilli powder
150g Spinach
100g smoked mackerel
2 eggs
  1. Start to cook the rice
  2. Sliced onion and fry on low until soft
  3. Start eggs boiling for 7 minutes
  4. Increase the heat, and add the mustard seeds to the frying pan. When they start to pop, add the chilli powder and turmeric. Fry for a minute. Add the spinach and flake in the mackerel, and fry until cooked. Add the rice once it is ready
