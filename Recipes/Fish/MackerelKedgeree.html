Mackerel Kedgeree
150g basmati rice 150g brown rice
1 onion, sliced
3 tsp mustard seeds
1.5 tsp turmeric powder
1.5 tsp chilli powder
150g Spinach
100g smoked mackerel
2 eggs
  1. Cook the rice according to the instructions
  2. Boil the eggs for 7 minutes, then remove the shell and quarter them
  3. Gently fry the onion in a little oil until soft but not brown
  4. Increase the heat and add the mustard seeds to the frying pan. When they start to pop, add the chilli powder and turmeric and fry for another half a minute
  5. Add the spinach to the pan, flake in the mackerel, and fry until the spinach has wilted
  6. Add the rice, and toss with the other ingredients. Serve, with the quartered eggs on top
Notes
  • Probably better with rice that has been cooked the day before
