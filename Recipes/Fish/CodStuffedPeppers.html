Peppers Stuffed With Salt Cod
Peppers
60g Onion
1 Garlic clove
30ml olive oil
400g salt cod (Soaked for 24hrs to reduce salt)
25g flour
100g Prawns
425ml Milk
12 Piquillo peppers
White pepper
Sauce
40g Onion
1 Garlic clove
15ml olive oil
8 Piquillo peppers
325ml full cream
  1. In a pan, fry the onion and garlic in the oil for the peppers until soft
  2. In a second pan, do the same with the onion and garlic for the sauce
  3. Flake the cod, add to the first pan, and fry until opaque
  4. Meanwhile, finish the sauce. Add the cream and peppers to the second pan, and cook until the cream has thickened quite a bit. Blend the sauce until smooth, and check for seasoning
  5. When the fish is cooked, add the flour, and prawns and cook for a few minutes
  6. Then gradually add the milk and cook for a few minutes until thickened. Check for salt and pepper
  7. Stuff the peppers with the cod mixture, arrange in a baking dish, and cover with the sauce
  8. Bake for 15 minutes at 180C
