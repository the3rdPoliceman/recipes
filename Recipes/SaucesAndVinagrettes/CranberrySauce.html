Cranberry Sauce
350g cranberries
225g sugar
150ml orange juice
zest of 1 orange
75 ml water
45 ml port
1 cinnamon stick
  1. Place all the ingredients in a pan over moderate heat. Bring to the boil, then simmer for 20 min
