Preserved Lemons
5 lemons
4 tablespoons salt
150-200ml lemon juice (enough to cover the lemons in the jar)
1 sprig rosemary
2 sprigs thyme
10 peppercorns
1 chilli

  5. After 3 more weeks they'll be ready to use
