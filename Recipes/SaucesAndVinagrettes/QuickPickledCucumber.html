Quick Pickled Cucumber
250ml rice wine vinegar (also worked fine with cheap white vinegar)
250ml water
150g sugar
1 tablespoon course salt
1 Cucumber
Good with carrots too
  1. Heat everything up except the cucumber, until everything dissolves
  2. Finely slice the cucumber, and add it to the pickle liquid
  3. Strain away the liquid after 10 minutes
