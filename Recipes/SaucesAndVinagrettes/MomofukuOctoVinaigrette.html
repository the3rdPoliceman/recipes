Momofuku Octo Vinaigrette
5 cloves garlic
30g ginger
1 chilli
60ml rice wine vinegar
60ml light soy sauce
30ml peanut oil rapeseed oil, sunflower oil
1/4 tsp sesame oil
10g sugar
black pepper
  1. Chop the garlic and ginger into cubes, roughly 2mm, and chop the chilli finely
  2. Mix everything together. Taste and add a bit more sugar if it needs it
  3. Best used fresh, but it’ll keep for a few days in the fridge
