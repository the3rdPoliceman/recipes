Chimichurri
120ml water
10g salt
6 cloves garlic
40g parsley
40g oregano (fresh)
1/2 tsp chili flakes
30ml red wine vinegar
60ml olive oil
  1. Dissolve the salt in the water
  2. Discard the oregano stalks, and the thicker parsley stalks
  3. Mince the garlic, and chop the oregano leaves and parsley very finely
  4. Mix everything together
Notes
  • Ideally this should be made at least a day in advance
  • Can be keep for a few weeks in the fridge
