Momofuku Fried Chicken
1.5 Kg Chicken Pieces
Octo Vinaigrette
Brine
1 liter lukewarm water
100g sugar
120g kosher salt
  1. Brine the Chicken for 1-6 hrs. (Tried 2hrs with small chicken legs - Not too salty, would go longer next time)
  2. Steam the Chicken for 20-40 minutes depending on the size of the pieces and put in fridge to chill for 2-24 hrs
  3. Take the chicken out of the refrigerator 1-2 hours before you fry it
  4. Dry the chicken and deep fry at 190C until the skin is deep drown and crisp, 6 to 8 minutes. Drain on kitchen paper and serve with the Vinaigrette
