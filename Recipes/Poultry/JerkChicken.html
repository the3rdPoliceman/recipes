Jerk Chicken
Brine
40 grams salt
30 grams sugar
1/2 tablespoon allspice (whole)
3 garlic cloves, crushed
1 Scotch bonnet pepper, halved Habanero
1.5inch piece ginger, roughly chopped
2 liters water
1 chicken, broken down
Jerk Paste (enough for at least 2 chickens)
100ml soy sauce
5 grams thyme leaves
100 ml Worcestershire sauce
30 grams tamarind paste
1.5 teaspoon ground allspice
1.5 teaspoon light brown sugar
5g salt
0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon
0.25 teaspoon ground cloves
3 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
3 spring onions, roughly chopped
1 scotch bonnet pepper, halved Habanero
1.5 piece ginger, peeled and roughly chopped
Jerk BBQ Sauce
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 tablespoons minced ginger
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 yellow onions, roughly chopped
600g ketchup
100 grams dark brown sugar
100 grams jerk paste
  1. Brine: Make the brine and brine the chicken overnight
  2. Jerk BBQ Sauce: Fry the ginger, garlic and onion in the oil until soft. Add the rest and simmer gently until thickened (about 1 hour). Blend smooth
  3. Jerk Paste: Blend all the ingredients until fairly smooth
  4. Drain & dry the chicken, then cover in jerk paste and marinade overnight
  5. Bake or grill the chicken at high heat, for 10 minutes. (ideally you want to grill at high heat with wood chips)
  6. Continue baking until cooked, basting a few times with the bbq sauce
