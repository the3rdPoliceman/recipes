Hungarian Chicken
4 chicken thighs
1 tablespoon olive oil
1/3 large onion, finely sliced
50g mushrooms, thickly sliced
500ml chicken stock
200g Creme Fraiche
2 tsp sweet paprika
1/8 tsp chilli flakes
Noodles
Probably worth trying the soy sauce, lemon juice, parsley from the hungarian mushroom soup
Can be done with or without chicken skin. If with skin, the sauce should be poured around the chicken to keep the skin crisp
  1. Heat oven to 175C
  2. Heat oil in casserole on medium and fry chicken until its nicely brown
  3. At the same time, in a frying pan, fry the onions gently for five minutes. Add the mushrooms and give 5 minutes more
  4. Add the stock, creme fraiche, paprika, chilli. Combine and correct salt
  5. If theres a lot of fat in the chicken, remove most of it. Then pour the sauce over the chicken, sprinkle more paprika on top, and bake until chicken
  6. is cooked (about 25 minutes)
  7. Serve with dill on top
