3 cup chicken
45ml toasted sesame oil
6 cloves garlic, peeled
2 inch piece of ginger, peeled and cut in discs
700g chicken cut in pieces
60ml light soy sauce
60ml mirin
20g thai basil italian basil
  1. In a pan, fry the ginger and garlic in the oil until fragrant
  2. Add the chicken and fry over high heat until it starts to brown
  3. Add the soy and mirin, and simmer until reduced to a syrupy consistency
  4. Mix in the basil at the last minute and serve on plain rice
