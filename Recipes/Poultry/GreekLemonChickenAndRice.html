Greek Lemon Chicken & Rice
2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp dried minced onion
4-5 cloves garlic, minced
zest of 1 lemon
1/2 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
olive oil
2 Chicken legs
1 large onion, peeled and diced
250ml long grain rice 250ml basmati rice
500ml chicken stock
lemon slices
fresh minced parsley, for garnish
extra lemon zest, for garnish
  1. Mix all the first ingredients into a marinade, adding enough olive oil to make a loose paste. Marinade the chicken for 15 minutes to 24 hours
  2. Clean the marinade off(save it) the chicken and brown in frying pan, then remove. Add the onion to the pan and fry until soft, add the marinade then fry another minute. Add the stock, bring to the boil and mix in the rice
  3. Transfer to oven dish if needed, put chicken and sliced lemon on top. Bake covered at 180 for 40 minutes, uncovering for the last 10 15 minutes
  4. Add parsley and lemon zest before serving
