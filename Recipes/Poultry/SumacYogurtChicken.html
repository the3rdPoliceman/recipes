Sumac & Yogurt Marinated Chicken
100ml yogurt
3 cloves garlic
2 shallots
3 tbsp sumac
1 tsp ground cumin
zest of 1 lemon
5g salt
20ml oil
1/2 tsp black pepper
4 chicken legs
  1. Blend all the ingredients to create a marinade, then cover the chicken with it
  2. Leave the marinated chicken in the fridge for at least an hour (overnight is better)
  3. Place the chicken on a baking tray (don't rub the marinade off), and sprinkle a little more sumac over it
  4. Bake at 200C until cooked through, about 30 minutes
