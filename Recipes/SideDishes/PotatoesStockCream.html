Potatoes in Stock and Cream
5 Potatoes, Peeled & Sliced 6mm thick
Sprig of Thyme
40g Butter
1/4 Onion, finely sliced
1/2 Leek, sliced
300ml Stock
150ml Cream
  1. Fry the onion and leek in half the butter until soft, then take off the heat
  2. Meanwhile, fry the potatoes & thyme in the rest of the butter for 5-10 minutes, or until it starts sticking
  3. Add the stock to the potatoes, cover, and cook, stirring occasionally, until it is all absorbed
  4. Top up with water a little as needed, and keep cooking with the cover on until the potatoes are cooked through, and no water remains
  5. Add the cream, leek and onion to the potatoes
  6. Check the seasoning, and finish in the oven for 15 minutes
