Sushi Rice
Rice
400g Sushi Rice
430g water
1 piece of kombu, 10x12cm
Vinegar Seasoning
25g sugar
42g vinegar
12g salt
  1. Wash the rice in cold water (warm water will result in less sticky rice) until the water runs clear, and leave to drain in a colander (ideally for 30mins+)
  2. Put the cold water and rice in a pan, with the kombu on top if using, covered with a tight lid and bring to the boil
  3. Let the rice boil hard for 2 minutes, then turn the heat as low as possible and leave for 15 minutes
  4. While the rice is cooking, heat the vinegar, sugar and salt slowly until everything is dissolved. Let cool
  5. Take the rice off the heat, cover with a cloth and let stand for 10 minutes (optional)
  6. Empty the rice (leave the crusty stuff at the bottom of the pan) onto a large surface like a baking sheet and mix in the vinegar mix. You may not need it all.
  7. If not using rice straight away, cover with a damp cloth. Do not refrigerate
