Naan Bread
1 tsp dry yeast
1 tsp sugar
70ml warm water (40C)
150g white flour
0.5 tsp salt
2 tbsp natural yoghurt
2 tbsp melted ghee butter
2 tsp nigella seeds
  1. Mix the yeast with the water and sugar and leave until it foams
  2. Mix the salt and seeds into the flour
  3. Combine the yeast mix, yoghurt, and butter and mix into the flour
  4. Knead until smooth (it'll still be fairly wet), then oil the surface and leave to double somewhere warm
  5. Get a frying pan super hot. A hotter pan gives much better air blisters in the bread
  6. Divide the dough into 4 and roll out thin (the seeds really help to get even thickness). You'll need more flour
  7. Dry fry until cooked on first side, flip and let the other side brown
  8. Take out and brush with melted butter
