Mushroom Croquetas
1630ml milk
5 bay leaves
6 cloves
140g butter
2/3 large onion, finely chopped
225g mushrooms, finely chopped
120g flour
1ml ground white pepper
14g salt
1tsp powdered cepes
2 eggs
200 g breadcrumbs
vegetable oil for deep-frying
  1. Bring milk to the boil along with bay and cloves, then leave to infuse
  2. In a separate pan, fry the onion in butter until soft, then add the mushrooms and fry another few minutes
  3. Add the flour, and fry a few minutes more
  4. Add the milk, pepper
  5. Weigh, then cook, stirring and scraping the base of the saucepan every other minute, until 1250g remains in the pan
  6. Stir in the powdered cepes
  7. Leave to chill overnight
  8. Ball, flour, egg, breadcrumb, and fry at 190C until brown
