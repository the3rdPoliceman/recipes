Gratin Potatoes
1Kg Waxy Potatoes (Charlotte?)
2 Medium Onions
300ml Milk
300ml Full Cream
5 Cloves Roasted Garlic 1 Clove Raw Garlic
1 Small bunch of Thyme
4 Bay Leaves
Nutmeg
  1. Fry the Onions until they are soft but not brown
  2. Put the Milk, Cream, Thyme, and Bay leaves in a pan and warm for 10 minutes - do not boil. Strain the milk to remove the bits and pieces, add the Garlic and blend
  3. Slice the Potatoes as thin as crisps, and layer in an oven proof dish with the Onions adding Salt & Pepper with each layer
  4. Cover with Cream, and bake at 190 for 1.5 - 2 hours
  5. It will be roughly the same temperature as Lava when it comes out of the oven
