Sauteed Potates
Waxy Potatoes (Charlotte is good, preferably around the size of an egg)
Rosemary
Garlic - One Clove per person
Duck fat neutral oil
Butter
  1. Cut the Potatoes in half and add to a pan of cold salted water. Boil gently until you can poke a fork into them without it feeling crunchy. Drain the Potatoes and leave to dry for 5 minutes or more
  2. Heat some Oil (not too tight, but not a bloody great pool) in frying pan over a medium heat and fry the Potatoes cut side down with Salt & Pepper until golden brown
  3. Add in Rosemary, Garlic and Butter and fry for a couple more minutes, flipping the Potatoes regularly
  4. Drain on kitchen paper
  5. Eat
