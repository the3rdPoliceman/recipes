Croquetas de Jamon
1630ml milk
5 bay leaves
6 cloves
140g butter
2/3 large onion
220g serrano ham
120g flour
1/2 nutmeg, grated
1ml ground white pepper
1.5g salt
100g flour
2 eggs
200 g breadcrumbs
vegetable oil for deep-frying
  1. Bring milk to the boil along with bay and cloves, then leave to infuse while preparing the onions and ham
  2. In a separate pan, fry the finely chopped onion in butter until soft, then add the ham and fry another minute
  3. Add the flour and fry for a few minutes
  4. Add the milk, nutmeg, and pepper
  5. Weigh, then cook, stirring constantly, until it loses 600g
  6. Add the salt gradually, while tasting
  7. Leave to chill overnight, then form into balls and coat with flour, eggs and breadcrumbs
  8. Fry until well browned
Notes
  • Try with egg yolks in the bechamel. Whisk 2 egg yolks and mix in a little of the bechamel, then put back into the rest of the bechamel
