Better Basmati Rice
200g Basmati Rice
1/2 Onion
2 Star Anise
1 Cinnamon Stick
3 Cardamom pods
350ml Half strength chicken stock
Salt
  1. Rinse the rice in a sieve and soak it in a bowl of cold water for half an hour
  2. In a pan, fry the onion in neutral oil until soft, then add the spices and fry a bit more
  3. Drain the rice and add to the pan, along with the stock
  4. Season and bring the to the boil, then cover with a tight fitting lid
  5. Put the covered pan in the oven set to 180C for 20 minutes
