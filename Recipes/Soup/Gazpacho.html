Gazpacho
55ml sherry vinegar apple cider vinegar
1kg tomatoes
50g onion
200g red pepper (about 1)
200g green pepper (about 1)
1 clove garlic
75g cucumber (about 0.25)
1 slice of bread
200g ice cubes 200ml cold water
100-150ml olive oil (more gives a creamier gazpacho)
12g salt
  1. Crush the garlic and roughly chop all the other vegetables
  2. Put half the vinegar, and all the other ingredients in a blender and blend thoroughly until smooth
  3. Taste for salt and vinegar, and adjust as necessary
  4. Ice, croutons, diced cucumber, peppers, onion, drizzled olive oil can be added as toppings when serving
Notes
  • It's important to keep everything cold. So best start with everything from fridge, and if possible to use ice rather than cold water
  • Typically I end up using all the vinegar
