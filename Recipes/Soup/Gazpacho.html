Gazpacho
1kg tomatoes
1/2 onion, roughly chopped
1 red pepper, seeded and chopped
1 green pepper, seeded and chopped
1 clove garlic
1/4 cucumber, chopped
1 slice of bread
200g ice cubes 200ml cold water
100-150ml olive oil (more gives a creamier gazpacho)
60ml sherry vinegar
1tsp salt
It's important to keep everything cold. So best start with everything from fridge, and if possible to use ice rather than cold water
  1. Put half the vinegar, and all the other ingredients in a blender and blend thoroughly until it is smooth
  2. Taste for salt and vinegar, and adjust as necessary
  3. Ice, croutons, diced cucumber, peppers, onion, drizzled olive oil can be added as toppings when serving
