Brocolli, Cheese & White Wine Soup
1 Small onion chopped
30g butter
1 head of broccoli
300 ml dry white wine
300 ml chicken stock
150 ml milk
150 g Blue Cheese
50g toasted walnuts
  1. Fry the onion in butter
  2. When it softens, add the brocolli and fry for a few minutes
  3. Add the wine and boil for a few minutes, then add the chicken stock and milk
  4. Cover and simmer until the brocolli
  5. Add in half the cheese, then puree the soup and season
  6. Serve with the rest of the cheese and toasted walnuts on top
