Wild Garlic Soup
30g butter
1 onion
3 small potatoes
1 colander of wild garlic
750ml chicken stock
2 shavings of lemon zest
nutmeg
50g sour cream
  1. Peel and slice the potatoes and boil in the stock, with the lemon peel
  2. Fry the onion in butter until soft, then add the wild garlic, nutmeg, salt and pepper, and fry until wilted
  3. Once the potatoes are done, add in the wild garlic, and blend
  4. Serve with a dollop of sour cream
