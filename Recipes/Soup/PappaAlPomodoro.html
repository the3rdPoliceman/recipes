Pappa Al Pomodoro
3 cloves garlic
1 large bunch of basil
500g cherry tomatoes
quality extra virgin olive oil
2 tins tomatoes
500g stale bread
  1. Roast the cherry tomatoes with some oil, salt, pepper, a bit of the basil and one sliced clove of garlic
  2. Slice the rest of the garlic and fry for minute along with the basil stalks
  3. Add the tinned tomatoes,and 1 tin of water. Bring to the boil, then simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Tear in the bread up, mix well and season to taste
  5. Tear in most of the remaining basil leaves and let the soup sit on a low heat for 10 minutes
  6. Add in the cherry tomatoes from the oven
  7. Add water to the soup if you need to (it should be like porridge)
  8. Remove from the heat, add 6 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil and serve with basil
