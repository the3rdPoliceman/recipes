Hungarian Mushroom Soup
2 onions
300g mushrooms
1 bunch of dill
600ml stock
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon paprika
30g butter
3 tablespoons flour
300ml milk
1 teaspoon salt
black pepper
2 teaspoons lemon juice
150ml sour cream
1 bunch of parsley
  1. Slice and fry the onions until softened
  2. Next add the mushrooms, 1 tsp dill, 100ml of the stock, the soy sauce, and the paprika. Cover and simmer 15 minutes
  3. Make a roux with the butter, flour and milk. Then stir the mushroom mix, and the rest of the stock into the roux
  4. Cover and simmer 10-15 minutes
  5. Just before serving, add salt, pepper, lemon juice, sour cream, parsley and the rest of the dill
