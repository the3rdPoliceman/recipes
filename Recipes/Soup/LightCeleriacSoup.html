Light Celeriac Soup
1 Celeriac
1 Small Onion
1 clove garlic
Stock
Cream
Milk
Crispy Bacon
  1. Fry Onion and Garlic until soft. Add Celeriac and fry for a few minutes. Add equal parts Stock, Milk and Cream to cover the Celeriac and cook until soft
  2. Blend the soup at high speed for a few minutes until it gets light and slightly foamy. Reheat and serve with the Bacon
