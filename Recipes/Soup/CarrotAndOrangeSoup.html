Carrot and Orange Soup
600g carrots
2 small onions
200g potatoes
spices
cream
1 orange
stock
  1. Roast carrots and onions
  2. Add to saucepan with potatoes and stock
  3. Bring to the boil and cook until potatoes are done
  4. Blend and add spices, orange juice to taste
  5. Add some cream & season
Serving Suggestions
  • Serve with sour cream, mixed with very finely sliced or grated orange zest
  • Finely grate some fresh ginger on top
