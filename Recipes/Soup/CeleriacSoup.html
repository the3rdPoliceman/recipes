Celeriac Soup
Butter
1 Celeriac
1 Potato
1 Leek
1 Onion
1 Clove Garlic
Stock
Pancetta
  1. Roughly chop vegetables and slice the garlic. Add to pan with the butter and fry until they soften
  2. Add the stock, and simmer until the celeriac is completely tender. Liquidise
  3. Fry the pancetta until crisy and use as garnish when serving
