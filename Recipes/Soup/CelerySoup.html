Celery Soup
1 head of celery, including leaves
1 large potato
1 onion
100g butter
750ml chicken stock (maybe vegetable is better)
1 bunch of dill
150ml heavy cream
  1. Dice celery, potatoes, and onions and cook in butter for 15 minutes, along with some salt
  2. Add the stock and cook for 10 more minutes
  3. Add dill, and a handful of the celery leaves and blend
  4. Stir in the cream, and season
