Sausage Rolls with Mustard
small sausage rolls
1 medium onion
6 large sausages
Zest of 1 Lemon
1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
1-2 tbsp chopped thyme
1 bunch of parsley
1/4 teaspoon pepper
640g puff pastry
1 egg, beaten
nigella seeds sesame seeds
  1. Chop the onion finely, fry until soft, then leave to cool
  2. Mix sausage chilled onion, meat, zest, mustard, thyme, parsley and pepper (maybe with handheld mixer)
  3. Divide each pastry sheet into long strips about 12cm wide (to initially form long sausage rolls which will then be chopped up into small ones)
  4. Make a line of sausage mix along the middle of each strip, egg wash one side, and roll the other side over
  5. Crimp the edge, eggwash the whole thing, sprinkle with seeds, and cut into individual sausage rolls
