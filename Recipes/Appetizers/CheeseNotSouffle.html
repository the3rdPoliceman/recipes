Cheese Not Souffle
4 eggs
150ml cream
black pepper
salt
pinch of cayenne pepper
butter
150g cheese (cheddar, gruyere etc. is good)
  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and add the cream, salt, pepper and cayenne pepper. Beat lightly with a whisk
  2. Butter a dish (a 25x15cm dish is good for 4 eggs), pour in the egg mixture, and grate in the cheese
  3. Bake at 180C for around 20 minutes, until the outside puffs up and solidifies, and the middle is still gooey
Notes
  • This is like a puffy, gooey centered quiche without the pastry. As with quiche, almost anything can be added
Variations
  • Asparagus and Goats Cheese. When adding the cheddar, add some asparagus spears that have been boiled for a couple of minutes in salted water, and top with chunks of goats cheese
  • Feta and Chilli. Add some finely chopped red chilli when mixing the egg, and top with feta at the end
