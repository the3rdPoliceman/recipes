Smoked Mackerel on Toast
2 Smoked Mackerel Fillets
50-60g Soured Cream
1 Lemon
Bread
Cayenne
Chives
  1. Make a Pate by blending one of the mackerel fillets with a few teaspoons of lemon juice and enough sour cream to give a nice consistency. Season, and adjust Lemon Juice to taste
  2. Slice and toast the bread, then spread a decent amount of Pate each piece. Flake the other Mackerel Fillet and put some on top. Dust with cayenne, sprinkle with finely chopped Chives and season with black pepper
