Chicken Liver Pate
180g butter
1 shallot
1 tsp thyme leaves
450g chicken livers
90ml cognac
40ml double cream
6g tsp salt
1/3 tsp ground allspice
1/3 tsp ground ginger
3 tsp green peppercorns
  1. Fry the shallot and thyme with a knob of the butter until soft, then put into blender
  2. Fry chicken livers until browned, but pink inside. Add to blender
  3. Add cognac to pan and reduce to syrup. Add to the blender
  4. Add the remaining butter, cream, salt, allspice and ginger to the blender
  5. Blend until smooth, then pass through a sieve
  6. Fold in the peppercorns and transfer to final dish
