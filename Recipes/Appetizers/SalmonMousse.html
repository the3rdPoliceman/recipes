Smoked Salmon Mousse
300g smoked salmon
300-400g crème fraîche
100g cream cheese
2 tbsp lemon juice
dill, finely chopped
salt and pepper
Not totally sure about creme fraiche amount, try and then correct recipe.
  1. Roughly chop the salmon, mix with everything else, and attack with a handblender to get a finer mousse
  2. Season with salt pepper and extra lemon if needed
