Cheese and Parsley Choux Buns
golf ball sized buns
125g butter
340ml water
0.5 teaspoon fine salt
0.25 teaspoon black pepper
250g flour
7 eggs
200g grated hard mozzarella
20g grated Parmesan
25g finely chopped parsley
  1. Heat oven to 200c Fan
  2. Heat the butter, water, salt and pepper in a non stick saucepan over medium best, until the butter melts
  3. Remove from the heat, chuck in all the flour and stir to mix quickly
  4. Put the pan back over a low medium heat and stir briskly with a wooden spoon for about 4 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough to a bowl and to cool down for a couple of minutes
  6. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating until conbined with the wooden spoon each time
  7. Adi the mozzarella, Parmesan and Parsley and mix
  8. Leave the mature to cool completely
  9. Place the dough into piping bag with a large nozzle
  10. Pipe 3cm balls onto baking paper on over tray, leaving 2cm between
  11. Bake until golden, around 25 minutes
