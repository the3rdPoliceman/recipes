Basic Agnolotti Dough
200g Flour
130g eggs
large pinch of salt
Normally for 200g flour i'd use 3 eggs yolks and add enough of the whites to get to 130g egg in total. It's flexible though
  1. Make a well from the flour and put eggs and salt in the middle
  2. Whisk the eggs with a fork, gradually incorporating flour until it's dry enough so you can work it and keep your hands pretty clean
  3. Knead, until smooth
  4. Roll
