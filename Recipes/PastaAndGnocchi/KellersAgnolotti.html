Agnolotti Dough (Thomas Keller)
225g Flour
1 Egg
6 Egg Yolks
5g olive oil
15g milk
  1. Make a well from the flour and put every thing in the middle
  2. Whisk it around with your fingers gradually incorporating everything
  3. Knead, until smooth
  4. Roll
