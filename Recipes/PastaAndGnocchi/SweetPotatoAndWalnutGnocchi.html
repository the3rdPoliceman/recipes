Sweet Potato and Walnut Gnocchi
Sweet Potato
Gnocchi
Walnuts
Cream
Cured Ham
Parmesan
Parsley
  1. Peel and dice the sweet potato, roast with salt pepper and olive oil until cooked. Add the walnuts and cook until browned slightly
  2. Chop or tear the ham, chop the parsley and shave the parmesan
  3. Boil the gnocchi, transfer to frying pan and fry with butter until browning slightly, add in the squash, walnuts, parsley, cream and some ham. Put onto plates. Warm a little more cream, and drizzle over along with remaining ham, and cheese
Variations
