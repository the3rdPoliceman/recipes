Courgette and Basil Pasta
½ Courgette
½ Clove Garlic
Olive Oil
Cream
Basil
Parmesan
  1. Chop Courgette into cigarettes and thinly slice the Garlic. Fry Courgette, with Salt & Pepper. Add the sliced garlic after a couple of minutes, and fry until courgette starts to color
  2. Add splash of Cream to the pan and allow to thicken a bit, then mix in Basil leaves once the sauce looks good
  3. Serve with Parmesan
