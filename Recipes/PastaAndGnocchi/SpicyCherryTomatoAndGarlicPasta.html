Spicy Cherry Tomato and Garlic Pasta
20 Cherry Tomatoes
4 cloves garlic, left whole
1 Dried Chili, left whole
Cream
Basil
Parmesan
Olive Oil
  1. Heat a good layer of Olive Oil in frying pan over medium heat
  2. Add the Tomatoes, Garlic and Chili and fry with a lid on the pan, swirling frequently until the Tomatoes start to soften and their skins begin to split. Remove the Chili, add a splash of Cream and the Basil
  3. Mix with the cooked Pasta and serve with Parmesan
  4. To control spicyness, the Chili can be removed earlier
