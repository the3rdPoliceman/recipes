Lasagna all'Emiliana
125g butter
Basil
4 cloves garlic
2 tins tomatoes
125g flour
1/2 onion
2 Bay leaves
1 Liter milk
100g sausage
1 teaspoon oregano
50g parmesan
100g gruyere
Lasagna sheets
250g cheap mozzarella
  1. Get 3 saucepans and a frying pan heating
  2. Saucepan 1, add butter, garlic and basil, and heat gently
  3. Saucepan 2, add tomatoes, and reduce excess water
  4. Saucepan 3, add milk and bayleaf, and bring just below boiling
  5. Frying pan, add meat, and brown, adding oregano for the last few minutes
  6. After 10 minutes, remove the basil and garlic from the butter. Then use the flour and milk to make a bechamel
  7. Combine everything apart from the sheets and mozzarella into the bechamel
  8. Layer it up, 6 layers sauce, 5 layers pasta, with mozzarella on top and bake for 30 minutes
