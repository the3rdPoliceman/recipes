Sweet Potato Agnolotti
(as starter)
Sweet Potato Filling
1200g sweet potatoes
100g unsalted butter
4 slices bacon
1/4 teaspoon grated nutmeg
Kosher salt and black pepper
Sage Cream
10 large sage leaves, chopped (save the smaller leaves for the fried sage leaf garnish)
200g creme fraiche
200g Buerre Monte (below)
Pinch of Kosher salt (to taste)
Canola oil for deep-frying
48 tiny sage leaves (reserved from above)
25g unsalted butter
4 thin slices prosciutto, cut crosswise into fine julienne
1 recipe Agnolotti
  1. Oven roast sweet potato
  2. Fry the bacon, and blend it to paste
  3. In a frying pan, mix the bacon, sweet potato flesh, nutmeg and butter. Fry for a while to reduce the moisture
  4. Chill, and then make the agnolotti
  5. For the sauce, make beurre monte, mix in warm creme fraiche, then blend along with Sage
  6. Serve with deep fried sage leaves and shredded ham
