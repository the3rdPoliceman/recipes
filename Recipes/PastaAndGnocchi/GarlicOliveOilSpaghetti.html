Garlic and Olive Oil Spaghetti
450 g Spaghetti
90ml extra virgin olive oil
3 cloves garlic, very finely chopped
1-2 Red Chillis (depends how hot they are)
1 bunch of parsley
  1. Add Oil, Garlic and Chilli to a saucepan, and heat gently until garlic has just a hint of color
  2. Cook the spaghetti, mix in the oil, and parsley
