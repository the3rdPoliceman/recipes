Prawn & Basil Pasta
Olive oil
Prawns
1/2 clove Garlic
Basil
Pine Nuts
  1. Make a loose pesto with the Basil, Oil and some Salt & Pepper
  2. Toast Pine Nuts
  3. Fry the Prawns and Garlic together, until the Prawns are cooked
  4. Mix together Prawns, Pesto, most of the Pine Nuts and Pasta
  5. Chuck some Pine Nuts on top with a bit of Basil when serving
Footer. Not much here really. Nice to have a footer though.
