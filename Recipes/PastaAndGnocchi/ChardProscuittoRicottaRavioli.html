Chard, Prosciutto & Ricotta Ravioli
Pasta
half recipe of basic agnolotti pasta
Filling
250g Chard Spinach
1‚ÅÑ2 small onion, finely chopped
3 slices prosciutto, finely chopped
25g butter
150g ricotta
1 egg yolk
30g grated parmesan
2 pinches grated nutmeg
Sauce
100ml cream
salt & pepper
20g toasted pine nuts
  1. Prepare the pasta
  2. Sweat the onion and proscuitto in the butter until cooked
  3. Add the spinach, fry and taste for salt
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta, egg yolk, parmesan and nutmeg
  5. Let the spinach cool for a few minutes, then mix everything together, and taste for salt & nutmeg
  6. Roll and stuff the pasta
  7. Cook the ravioli, and drain before adding to the sauce
  8. Warm the cream, season and add to the ravioli before serving
