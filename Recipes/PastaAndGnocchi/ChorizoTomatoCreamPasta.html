Chorizo, Tomato, Cream Pasta
Olive oil
1/2 tin tomatoes
1 clove Garlic
Chopped Rosemary
Chorizo
pinch of dried red chilli flakes
Cream
Pasta
Parmesan
  1. Oil, tomatoes, garlic and rosemary in the pan, with a bit of salt and pepper. Cook the tomatoes until they have begun to soften - about 5 minutes
  2. Add the sausage and chilli flakes, and enough cream to leave the sauce still rich in colour. Simmer very gently for about 10 minutes
  3. Parmesan on top
