Tomato and Pomegranate Salad
1kg tomatoes (mixed, best you can get)
1 red pepper
1 small red onion
1 clove of garlic
10ml red wine vinegar
25ml pomegranate molasses
60ml olive oil, plus a little extra to drizzle at the end
1 pomegranate
1 small bunch oregano leaves
Salt and pepper
  1. Cut the tomatoes and pepper into roughly 0.5cm dice, and chop the onion as finely as possible. Mix together in a large bowl
  2. Crush the garlic and combine with the vinegar, pomegranate molasses and oil, and stir into the tomato mix
  3. Taste for salt and vinegar. It'll probably need 2-3 grams of salt
  4. Seed the pomegranate
  5. Before serving, finely chop the oregano and stir into the tomatoes along with half the pomegranate seeds.Sprinkle the remaining seeds on top, and drizzle with a bit more olive oil
