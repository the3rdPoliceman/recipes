Blood orange and anchovy salad
200g fennel, thinly sliced
3 tbsp white-wine vinegar
100ml olive oil
coarse sea salt
black pepper
2 tsp fennel seeds
2 tsp coriander seeds
2 blood oranges
1 grapefruit
70g Kalamata olives, pitted
20g basil leaves, roughly shredded
30g anchovies, cut into 2cm-long pieces
Small bag rocket
10g tarragon leaves, roughly chopped
radishes, thinly sliced
  1. Peel and slice oranges and grapefruit
  2. Put half the vinegar, half the oil, some juice from grapefruit and orange and some salt with the fennel and leave for 15 minutes
  3. Toast fennel and coriander seeds in a pan and crush
  4. Add the remaining oil and vinegar, and all the other ingredients into the fennel, and toss. Check for salt
