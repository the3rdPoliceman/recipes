Roast peach, Parma ham & Mozzarella salad
6 peaches
extra virgin olive oil
250g buffalo mozzarella
12 slices Parma ham
1 lemon
sea salt
black pepper
100 g rocket
1 small bunch of mint
  1. Roast peaches for 20 minutes with a little oil. Make vinaigrette with lemon and oil, add to rocket, and chuck everything else on top
