Avocado & Orange Salad
2 avocados
0.5 lemon
sea salt
pepper
1 spring onion
1 orange
20ml olive oil
chive flowers
  1. Skin the avocado and chop in 1cm dice
  2. Mix with the lemon juice and season with a large pinch of salt and some pepper
  3. Use a sharp knife to cut the skin off the orange, and then cut out the segments of orange, so you remove all the pith
  4. Finely slice the green part of the spring onion, and mix it, the olive oil, and the orange in with the diced avocado
  5. Top with chive flowers, if using
