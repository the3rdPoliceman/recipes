Beetroot, Pear and Feta Salad
1 Small Beetroot (raw)
1 Pear
50g Feta
Mint
1 Lemon
Olive Oil
  1. Make a 3:1 Dressing with the Oil and Lemon, adding some Pepper
  2. Very thinly slice the Pear and Beetroot (Veggie Peeler works well). Mix it with a handful of chopped Mint, and some dressing
  3. Add the Feta on top when serving
