Celeriac Remoulade
as a side dish
1 Large Celeriac
120ml Mayo
120ml Creme Fraiche
2Tbsps Cornichons
2Tbsps Capers
2tsps Dijon
1tbsp Cider Vinegar
3tbsps tarragon chervil parsley and chives (in total)
Lemon
  1. Mix everything together except the Celeriac and Lemon and season. Put in fridge for 1hr-3days, the flavors will improve
  2. Chop the Celeriac into matchsticks or grate finely, add enough dressing to coat and refrigerate for 2 to 8 hours
  3. To serve, add more Dressing, Seasoning and Lemon Juice as you like
