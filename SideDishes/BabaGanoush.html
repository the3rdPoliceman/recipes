Baba Ganoush
Ingredients
3 Aubergines (1 kg)
50g Tahini
80ml extra-virgin olive oil
1 small bunch fresh parsley
30g yogurt
1-2 cloves garlic, crushed
25-40ml lemon juice
Kosher salt
pinch of cumin
pinch of cayenne
Directions
Try smoked salt
Try some yoghurt mixed in (seems to be quite common in traditional recipes). Up to 100g
Gets better after a day or 2
Burn the aubergine in the oven or grill until they're very soft inside and charred outside
Scrape the flesh out and remove excess water. This can be done by leaving in a sieve for half an hour or gently spinning in a salad spinner
Mix in the other tahini, oil, joghurt and parsley
Then add salt, garlic and lemon to taste
Drizzle with olive oil when serving
