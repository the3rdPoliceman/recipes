Sushi Rice
Ingredients
400g Sushi Rice
540g water
36g sugar
42g vinegar
11g salt
Directions
Rinse the rice until the water runs clear, and leave to drain in a colander
Put the cold water and rice in a pan, covered with a tight lid and bring to the boil
Let boil hard for 2 minutes, then turn the heat to lowest and leave for 15 minutes
While the rice is cooking, mix the vinegar, sugar and salt and heat slowly until dissolved. Let cool
Take the rice off the heat, cover with a cloth and let stand for 10 minutes (optional)
Empty the rice onto a large surface (baking sheet) and mix in the vinegar mix (you may not need it all)
