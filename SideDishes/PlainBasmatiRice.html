Plain Basmati Rice
Basmati Rice
Water
  1. Keys: 4:3 water to rice ratio by volume, or 7:4 by weight
  2. Rinse, Soak, Oven
  3. Rinse the rice in a sieve and soak it in a bowl of cold water for half an hour
  4. Drain and add to a pan along with 1.33X ratio of water to rice (dry rice volume)
  5. Season and bring the to the boil, then put a tight fitting lid on
  6. Put the covered pan in the oven set to 180C for 20 minutes
