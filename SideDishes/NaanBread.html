Naan Bread
Ingredients
1 tsp dry yeast
1 tsp sugar
70ml warm water (40C)
150g white flour
0.5 tsp salt
2 tbsp natural yoghurt
2 tbsp melted ghee or butter
2 tsp nigella seeds
Directions
Mix the yeast with the water and sugar and leave until it foams
Mix the salt and seeds into the flour
Combine the yeast mix, yoghurt, and butter and mix into the flour
Knead until smooth (it'll still be fairly wet), then oil the surface and leave to double somewhere warm
Get a frying pan super hot
Divide the dough into 4 and roll out thin (the seeds really help to get even thickness). You'll need more flour
Dry fry until cooked on first side, flip and let cook out
Brush with melted butter
