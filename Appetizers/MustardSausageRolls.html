Sausage Rolls with Mustard
50 small sausage rolls
Ingredients
1 medium onion
6 large sausages
Zest of 1 Lemon
1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
1-2 tbsp chopped thyme
bunch of parsley
1/4 teaspoon pepper
640g puff pastry (2 25x42 packs)
1 beaten egg
nigella or sesame seeds
Directions
Chop the onion finely, fry until soft, then leave to cool
Mix sausage chilled onion, meat, zest, mustard, thyme, parsley and pepper (maybe with handheld mixer)
Divide each pastry sheet into 2, along the length, so you have 4 strips in total, each 12.5cm by 42cm
Make a line of sausage mix along each strip, egg wash one side, and roll the other side over to make a single 42cm long sausage roll
Crimp the edge, eggwash the whole thing, sprinkle with seeds, and divide into individual sausage rolls
