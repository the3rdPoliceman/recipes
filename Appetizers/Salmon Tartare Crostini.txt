Salmon Tartare Crostini
Ingredients
Method
Make citrus marinated salmon from French Laundry, marinated but not cooked. Wash off the marinade and leave in fridge for flavours to spread through the fish. Mix in a few chives, and serve on toast, with either avocado puree or kewpie mayo 
Avocado Puree - Chefsteps have a recipe. Or blitz avocado with salt, lemon juice, pepper, and some oil until smooth. Possibly add some mayo
