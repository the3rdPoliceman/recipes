Gyoza
gyoza
Ingredients
150g cabbage, finely chopped
1 teaspoon sea salt
250g pork mince
1 cloves garlic, grated
50g garlic chives, finely minced scallions
10g ginger, finely grated
60 ml chicken stock dashi
7ml soy sauce
7ml sake
5ml sesame oil
black pepper
30 gyoza wrappers
15ml sesame oil
Directions
Rub the salt into the cabbage and leave it to draw water out, then squeeze out the water
Mix everything together very well, using only enough chicken stock to get it to mix nicely
Fry little bits to check for salt, and correct if necessary
Make the gyoza using water to seal. See here
Heat some neutral oil in a pan at medium high, add the gyoza, and fry one side until almost burning
Add a few millimetres of water to the pan, immediately cover with a lid and steam
The idea is that they should be cooked at the same point as th pan is dried
Serve drizzled with a little sesame oil, some soy, or other dipping sauce
