Smoked Salmon Mousse
Ingredients
300g smoked salmon
300-400g crème fraîche
100g cream cheese
2 tbsp lemon juice
Fresh dill, finely chopped
salt and pepper
Directions
Not totally sure about creme fraiche amount, try and then correct recipe.
Roughly chop the salmon, mix with everything else, and attack with a handblender to get a finer mousse
Season with salt pepper and extra lemon if needed
