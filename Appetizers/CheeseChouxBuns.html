Cheese and Parsley Choux Buns
40 golf ball sized buns
Ingredients
125g butter
340ml water
0.5 teaspoon fine salt
0.25 teaspoon black pepper
250g flour
7 eggs
200g grated hard mozzarella
20g grated Parmesan
25g finely chopped parsley
Directions
Heat oven to 200c Fan
Heat the butter, water, salt and pepper in a non stick saucepan over medium best, until the butter melts
Remove from the heat, chuck in all the flour and stir to mix quickly
Put the pan back over a low medium heat and stir briskly with a wooden spoon for about 4 minutes
Transfer the dough to a bowl and to cool down for a couple of minutes
Add the eggs, one at a time, beating until conbined with the wooden spoon each time
Adi the mozzarella, Parmesan and Parsley and mix
Leave the mature to cool completely
Place the dough into piping bag with a large nozzle
Pipe 3cm balls onto baking paper on over tray, leaving 2cm between
Bake until golden, around 25 minutes
